STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetocivilrightsenforcement.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Civil Rights Act; Civil Rights Enforcement. RSA 354-B:1 is repealed and reenacted to read as
2 follows:
3 354-B:1 CivilRightsEnforcement.
4 I.(a) Any person who, motivated in whole or in part by hostility, including but not limited to
5 animus, bias, or prejudice, toward the race, color, religion, national origin, ancestry, sexual
6 orientation, sex, gender identity, or disability of the person, group, or organization directly or
7 indirectly targeted by the unlawful act, or the affiliation or association of the person, group, or
8 organization directly or indirectly targeted by the unlawful act with a person or people of a certain
9 race, color, religion, national origin, ancestry, sexual orientation, sex, gender identity, or disability,
10 knowingly commits one of the following unlawful acts shall be subject to civil liability under RSA
11 354-B:2andtotheremediesprovidedinRSA354-B:3andRSA354-B:4:
12 (1) Usingunprivilegedforceagainstanother;
13 (2) Committinganactofviolenceagainstanother;
14 (3) Causingdamagetoortrespassingonthepropertyofanother;or
15 (4) Threateningtocommitanyoftheactslistedabove.
16 (b) In this section, “threatening to commit” means the communication, by physical
17 conduct or by declaration, of an intent to inflict harm or damage to person or property that, taken in
18 the context of surrounding circumstances, would convey to a reasonable person that the actor has
19 the apparent ability to carry out the threat and the real possibility that such harm or damage may
20 occur.
21 II. Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to prohibit any conduct or speech that is
22 protectedundertheUnitedStatesConstitutionortheNewHampshireconstitution.
23 III. It shall be unlawful for any person to interfere or attempt to interfere with the rights
24 securedbythischapter.
25 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
