STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishingthecyanobacteriamitigationloanandgrantfundtaskforce.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Section; Cyanobacteria Mitigation Loan and Grant Fund Task Force. Amend RSA 485-A
2 byinsertingaftersection61thefollowingnewsection:
3 485-A:62 CyanobacteriaMitigationLoanandGrantFundTaskForce.
4 I. There is established a task force to study developing a sustainable funding source for the
5 cyanobacteriamitigationloanandgrantfundestablishedinRSA485-A:58.
6 II. NotwithstandingRSA14:49themembersofthetaskforceshallbeasfollows:
7 (a) Twomembersofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
8 (b) Two members of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the house
9 ofrepresentatives.
10 (c) Thegovernor,ordesignee.
11 (d) Thecommissionerofthedepartmentoftransportation,ordesignee.
12 (e) Thedirectorofthedepartmentofenvironmentalservicessubsurfacesystemsbureau.
13 (f) Thedirectorofthedepartmentofenvironmentalserviceslimnologycenter.
14 (g) Theexecutivedirectorofthefishandgamedepartment,ordesignee.
15 (h) A member of the marine patrol, appointed by the director of the division of state
16 police.
17 (i) Amemberofariverorlakeassociation,appointedbythegovernor.
18 III. Legislative members of the task force shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when
19 attendingtothedutiesofthetaskforce.
20 IV. Thetaskforceshall:
21 (a) Explore including a voluntary decal program to be displayed on non-motorized
22 watercraftallowingpeopletodonatetothefundbyselectingacheckboxontheirmotorizedboatand
23 sailboat registrations and mooring decal permits. Such program shall be patterned after the New
24 HampshirevoluntaryhikesafecardunderRSA206:26-bb.
25 (b) Exploreestablishingalicenseplatedecal.
26 (c) Exploreothervoluntaryfundingsources.
27 (d) Explore the direction of a portion of the failed septic design application fee to be
28 transferedtothecyanobacteriamitigationloanandgrantfund.
29 (e) Explore reallocating a percentage, to be determined by the department of
30 transportation, of mitigation funds resulting from transportation drainage impacts that are
SB598 -ASINTRODUCED
-Page2-
1 currently allocated to the aquatic resource compensatory mitigation fund established in RSA 482-
2 A:29tothecyanobacteriamitigationloanandgrantfund.
3 (f) Explore the establishment of a program at the department of transportation for the
4 purpose of improving and/or upgrading culverts, drainage features, or other storm water
5 infrastructure in the same watershed as the impacts with the goal of reducing cyanobacteria in New
6 Hampshirewaterbodies.
7 (g) Explore any other issues the task force discovers may lead to a sustainable funding
8 sourceforthecyanobacteriamitigationloanandgrantfund.
9 V. The members of the study task force shall elect a chairperson from among the members.
10 The first meeting of the task force shall be called by the first-named senate member. The first
11 meeting of the task force shall be held within 45 days of the effective date of this section. Four
12 membersofthetaskforceshallconstituteaquorum.
13 VI. The task force shall report its findings and any recommendations for proposed
14 legislation to the president of the senate, the speaker of the house of representatives, the senate
15 clerk,thehouseclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
16 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
