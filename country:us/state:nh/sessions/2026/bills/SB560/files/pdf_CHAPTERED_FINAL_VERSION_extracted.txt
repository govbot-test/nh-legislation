ANACT modernizingtheNewHampshirecoordinatesystem.
SPONSORS: Sen. Ricciardi, Dist 9; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Rep. B. Boyd, Hills. 12; Rep. Veilleux,
Hills.34
COMMITTEE: Transportation
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill modernizes and clarifies the framework governing New Hampshire’s coordinate systems
used in land surveying and mapping by formally adopting the most recent coordinate system released by
theNationalGeodeticSurvey(NGS),updatingterminology,andestablishingusagerestrictions.

ANACT modernizingtheNewHampshirecoordinatesystem.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 196:1 New Paragraph; New Hampshire Coordinate System. Amend RSA 1-A:1 by inserting after
2 paragraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
3 III. The “New Hampshire state plane coordinate system” (NHSPCS) is identical to the New
4 Hampshire portion of the state plane coordinate system (SPCS) as defined by the National Geodetic
5 Survey (NGS) or its successors meaning the most recent system of plane coordinates which has been
6 officially released for public use by the National Geodetic Survey (NGS) or its successors, based on the
7 NationalSpatialReferenceSystem(NSRS)oritssuccessors.
8 196:2 ApplicationofCoordinateSystems. AmendRSA1-A:2toreadasfollows:
9 1-A:2 ApplicationofCoordinateSystems. Theuseoftheterm"NewHampshirecoordinatesystemof
10 1927" or "New Hampshire coordinate system of 1983" orthe"NewHampshirestateplanecoordinate
11 system"onanymap,reportofsurvey,orotherdocumentshallbelimitedtocoordinatesbasedontheNew
12 Hampshire coordinate system as defined in RSA 1-A:1. The New Hampshire coordinate system of 1927
13 shall not be used after December 31, 1989. The New Hampshire coordinate system of 1983 shallnotbe
14 [the sole coordinate system used on or after January 1, 1990] usedforprojectscommencedafterthe
15 "NewHampshirestateplanecoordinatesystem,"asdefinedinRSA1-A:1,IIIhasbeenreleasedforpublic
16 usebytheNationalGeodeticSurvey(NGS)oritssuccessors.
17 196:3 SystemsofPlaneCoordinates. AmendRSA1-A:3toreadasfollows:
18 1-A:3 Systems of Plane Coordinates. The systems of plane coordinates which have been
19 established by the National Ocean Service/National Geodetic Survey or its successors for defining and
20 stating the geographic positions or locations of points on the surface of the earth within the state of New
21 Hampshire are hereafter to be known and designated as the New Hampshire coordinate system of 1927
22 and the New Hampshire coordinate system of 1983,andtheNewHampshirestateplanecoordinate
23 system(NHSPCS).
24 196:4 PlaneCoordinateValues. AmendRSA1-A:4toreadasfollows:
25 1-A:4 PlaneCoordinateValues.
26 I. The planecoordinatevalues for apoint ontheearth's surface, usedto expressthe geographic
27 position or location of such point in this system, shall consist of 2 distances expressed in United States
28 survey feet,whichequals(1200)/(3937)meters,and decimals of a foot when using the New Hampshire
29 coordinate system of 1927 and expressed in meters and decimals of a meter [and] orUnited States
30 survey feet and decimals of a foot when using the New Hampshire coordinate system of 1983. One of
31 these distances, to be known as the "x-coordinate," shall give the position in an east-and-west direction;
32 theother, tobeknownasthe"y-coordinate,"shall givethepositioninanorth-and-southdirection. These

15 196:5 RecordingCoordinates RSA1-A:5isrepealedandreenactedtoreadasfollows:
16 1-A:5 Recording Coordinates. Coordinates based on any New Hampshire coordinate system,
17 purportingtodefinethepositionofapointonalandboundary,presentedtoberecordedinanypublicland
18 recordsordeedrecordsshallbeaccompaniedbyaspecificstatementbythelicensedlandsurveyorasto
19 their basis and a description of the survey method used to determine them on the record plat or
20 descriptionofthesurvey.
21 196:6 New Section; Restrictions. Amend RSA 1-A by inserting after section 5 the following new
22 section:
23 1-A:6 Restrictions. The use of the terms “New Hampshire state plane coordinate system,” “New
24 Hampshire coordinate system 1927,” or “New Hampshire coordinate system 1983” on any map, report of
25 survey, or other document shall be limited to coordinates based on the respective New Hampshire
26 coordinatesystemasdefinedinthischapter.
196:7 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
