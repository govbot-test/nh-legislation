648
ANACT requiring age verification by commercial entities to allow access to pornographic
material.
SPONSORS: Sen.Reardon,Dist15
COMMITTEE: Judiciary
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill requires a person that owns, operates, or controls a website or application for
commercial purposes and publishes or distributes a substantial portion of material that is
pornographic to implement age verification procedures. This bill also authorizes the attorney
general to enforce such requirements against commercial entities for violations of such
requirements.

1 1 New Chapter; Restriction on Access to Pornographic Material. Amend RSA by inserting after
2 chapter507-Ithefollowingnewchapter:
3 CHAPTER507-J
4 RESTRICTIONONACCESSTOPORNOGRAPHICMATERIAL
5 507-J:1 Definitions. Inthischapter:
6 I. "Pornographicmaterial"meansmaterialthatdepictsnudityorsexualactsforthepurpose
7 ofsexualstimulation.
8 (a) The presence of nudity or sexual acts in material shall not necessarily make that
9 material pornographic, if the purpose of that form of material is something other than sexual
10 stimulation.
11 (b) Pornographic material may take the form of photographs, videos, writings, audio
12 recordings,oranimation,amongothermediaformats.
13 II. "Commercialentity"meansanypersonorbusinessthat:
14 (a) Owns,operates,orcontrolsawebsiteorapplicationforcommercialpurposes;and
15 (b) Publishesordistributesasubstantialportionofpornographicmaterial.
16 III. "Substantial portion" means content in which more than one-half of a website's total
17 content,measuredbyvolumeornumberofimagesandvideos,ispornographicmaterial.
18 IV. "Reasonableageverificationmethod"means:
19 (a) Agovernment-issuedidentificationcardcheckconductedbyathird-partyverification
20 service;or
21 (b) Any other method that reasonably verifies that the user is at least 18 years of age,
22 consistentwithindustrybestpracticesandwithnoretentionofidentifyingpersonaldata.
23 507-J:2 AgeVerificationRequirement.
24 I. A commercial entity that knowingly and intentionally publishes or distributes
25 pornographicmaterialontheInternetshall:
26 (a) Require all users attempting to access such material to verify their age using a
27 reasonableageverificationmethod;
28 (b) Not retain any personal identifying information used during the verification process;
29 and
30 (c) Postaclearwarningmessageonthelandingpagethatageverificationisrequired.

1 507-J:3 State Enforcement. The attorney general may bring an action for injunctive relief and
2 civil penalties of up to $25,000 per violation against any commercial entity in violation of this
3 chapter.
4 507-J:4 DataPrivacy.
5 I. No commercial entity or third-party age verification provider shall retain, store, or sell
6 anypersonalinformationusedtoverifyageunderthischapter.
7 II. A knowing violation of this section shall be a separate civil violation subject to a penalty
8 of$50,000perincident.
9 507-J:5 Exemptions. Thischaptershallnotapplyto:
10 I. Newsorganizations.
11 II. Libraries.
12 III. Museums.
13 IV. Institutions of higher education when the material is distributed in good faith for
14 legitimateeducationalorscientificpurposes.
15 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
