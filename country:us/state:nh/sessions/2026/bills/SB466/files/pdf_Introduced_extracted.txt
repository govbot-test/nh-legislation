SB466-FN-ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION
26-2003
09/08
466-FN
ANACT relativetothepossessionofafirearmwithoutaserialnumber.
SPONSORS: Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. Birdsell, Dist 19; Sen. Lang, Dist 2; Sen. Pearl, Dist
17; Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Rep. Harvey-Bolia, Belk. 3; Rep. M. Pearson, Rock.
34;Rep.L.Walsh,Rock.15
COMMITTEE: Judiciary
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill makes it a class A felony to change, alter, remove or obliterate the name of the maker,
model,manufacturer'snumber,orothermarkofidentificationonafirearm.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SB466-FN-ASINTRODUCED
26-2003
09/
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetothepossessionofafirearmwithoutaserialnumber.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Public Safety and Welfare; Changing Marks on a Firearm. Amend RSA 159:13 to read as
2 follows:
3 159:13 Changing Marks. No person shall change, alter, remove, or obliterate the name of the
4 maker, model, manufacturer's number, or other mark of identification on any pistol or revolver, or
5 any other firearm. Possessionofanysuchfirearmsuponwhichthesameshallhavebeenchanged,
6 altered, removed, or obliterated shall be presumptive evidence that such possessor has changed,
7 altered, removed, or obliterated the same. Any person who violates the provisions of this section
8 shallbeguiltyofa[misdemeanor]classAfelony.
9 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
