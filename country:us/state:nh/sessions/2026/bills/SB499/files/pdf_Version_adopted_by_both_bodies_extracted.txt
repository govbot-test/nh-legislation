STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to the membership, duties, and reporting requirements of the traffic
safetycommission.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Subparagraph; Traffic Safety Commission; Membership. Amend RSA 21-P:64, I(b) by
2 insertingaftersubparagraph(11)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
3 (12) A member of the trauma medical review committee established under RSA
4 153-A:8,appointedbythecommittee.
5 2 New Paragraph; Traffic Safety Commission; Duty Added. Amend RSA 21-P:64 by inserting
6 afterparagraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
7 II-a. The commission shall review and analyze any available and relevant information and
8 datarelatedtothecauseoftrafficcollisionsinNewHampshire.
9 3 TrafficSafetyCommission;Reports. AmendRSA21-P:64,Vtoreadasfollows:
10 V. The commissionshall report annually, beginning November 1, 2018, tothespeaker of the
11 house of representatives, the president of the senate, and the governor. The reports shall describe
12 the activities and findings of the commission and any recommendations for proposed legislation.
13 Beginning November 1, 2026, the reports shall include aggregated data on the causes of
14 traffic collisions in New Hampshire for that year and recommendations on how to address
15 thesecauses.
16 4 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
