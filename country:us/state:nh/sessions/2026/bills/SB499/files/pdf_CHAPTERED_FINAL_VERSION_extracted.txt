ANACT relative to the membership, duties, and reporting requirements of the traffic safety
commission.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 135:1 New Subparagraph; Traffic Safety Commission; Membership. Amend RSA 21-P:64, I(b) by
2 insertingaftersubparagraph(11)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
3 (12) AmemberofthetraumamedicalreviewcommitteeestablishedunderRSA153-
4 A:8,appointedbythecommittee.
5 135:2 New Paragraph; Traffic Safety Commission; Duty Added. Amend RSA 21-P:64 by inserting
6 afterparagraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
7 II-a. The commission shall review and analyze any available and relevant information and data
8 relatedtothecauseoftrafficcollisionsinNewHampshire.
9 135:3 TrafficSafetyCommission;Reports. AmendRSA21-P:64,Vtoreadasfollows:
10 V. The commission shall report annually, beginning November 1, 2018, to the speaker of the
11 house of representatives, the president of the senate, and the governor. The reports shall describe the
12 activities and findings of the commission and any recommendations for proposed legislation. Beginning
13 November1,2026,thereportsshallincludeaggregateddataonthecausesoftrafficcollisionsinNew
14 Hampshireforthatyearandrecommendationsonhowtoaddressthesecauses.
135:4 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
Approved:June12,2026
EffectiveDate:January01,2027
