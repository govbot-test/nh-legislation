ANACT establishingthecommissionforchildren'sfutures.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Subdivision; Study Commission for Children's Futures. Amend RSA 200 by inserting
2 aftersection63thefollowingnewsubdivision:
3 StudyCommissionforChildren'sFutures
4 200:64 Commission Established. There is a commission established to study the health and
5 well-being of children in the state and to identify the gaps in mental and physical health services
6 provided to children. The commission shall be administratively attached to the department of
7 education.
8 I. NotwithstandingRSA14:49,themembersofthecommissionshallbeasfollows:
9 (a) Two members of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the house
10 ofrepresentatives;
11 (b) One member of the senate from the democratic party and one member of the senate
12 fromtherepublicanparty,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate;
13 (c) Twohealthcareprofessionalslicensedinthestate,appointedbythegovernor;
14 (d) One medical practitioner licensed in the state specializing in either traditional or
15 holisticmedicine,appointedbythespeakerofthehouse;
16 (e) Onechildpsychologistlicensedinthestate;appointedbythesenatepresident;
17 (f) Onementalhealthcareprovider,appointedbythespeakerofthehouse;
18 (g) Two teachers certified by the department of education, one of whom is certified in
19 specialeducation,appointedbythegovernor;
20 (h) Onedietitian,asdefinedinRSA326-H:3,appointedbythesenatepresident;
21 (i) Onemember ofstateorlocal lawenforcement, appointedbythespeaker ofthehouse;
22 and
23 (j) Three parents of children residing in the state, one of whom is appointed by the
24 governor,oneofwhomisappointedbythespeakerofthehouse,andoneofwhomisappointedbythe
25 senatepresident.
26 II. Legislative members of the commission shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when
27 attendingtothedutiesofthecommission.
28 III. Thecommissionshallstudy:
29 (a) The usage of individualized education programs (IEPs) in schools and trends within
30 IEPsovertime;
