SB561-ASINTRODUCED
26-2157
08/06
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetochangeofaddressforapplicantsforagamingfacilitylicense.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Paragraph; Facilities License Application; Change of Address. Amend RSA 287-D:7 by
2 insertingafterparagraphIIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
3 IV.(a) An applicant for a facility license with a pending application may amend the
4 applicationtoreflectachangeinthephysicaladdressoftheproposedfacility,providedthat:
5 (1) ThenewaddressislocatedwithinthestateofNewHampshire;and
6 (2) The municipality in which the new address is located has not voted to prohibit
7 theoperationofgamesofchancepursuanttoRSA287-D:4,IIorotherapplicablelaw.
8 (b) The applicant shall notify the lottery commission in writing of the new address and
9 submitanydocumentationrequiredbythecommissiontodemonstratecompliancewiththissection.
10 (c) Upon receipt of such notice, the lottery commission shall have not less than 90 days
11 toevaluatethenewaddressandincorporateitsfindingsintothedecisiononthependingapplication.
12 (d) A change of address while an application pending shall not require filling out a new
13 application.
14 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
