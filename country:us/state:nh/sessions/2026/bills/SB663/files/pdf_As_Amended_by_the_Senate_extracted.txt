IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT creating a Medicaid methodology working group within the department of health
andhumanservices.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Department of Health and Human Services; Medicaid Methodology Working Group
2 Established.
3 I. Thedepartmentofhealthandhumanservicesshallconveneaworkinggroupforthestudy
4 ofnursinghomerateMedicaidmethodologyconsistingofthefollowing:
5 (a) A member of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the house of
6 representatives;
7 (b) Amemberofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate;
8 (c) Thecommissionerofthedepartmentofhealthandhumanservices,ordesignee;
9 (d) A member of the New Hampshire Association of Counties, appointed by that
10 organization;
11 (e) A member of the New Hampshire Health Care Association, appointed by that
12 organization;
13 (f) A member of the New Hampshire Hospital Association, appointed by that
14 organization;and
15 (g) A certified public accountant specializing in nursing home cost reporting, appointed
16 bythegovernor.
17 II. The working group shall be convened by the commissioner of department of health and
18 human services, or their designee, and shall be subject to RSA 91-A. The working group shall make
19 recommendations concerning the nursing home reimbursement methodology, with a focus on the
20 followingconsiderations:
21 (a) Accesstonursinghomecareandtheexistenceofwaitinglists;
22 (b) Barrierstohospitaldischargetonursinghomes;
23 (c) Whether the acuity or direct care needs of nursing home residents are properly
24 capturedbyreimbursement;
25 (d) Capitalreimbursementinlightofagingnursinghomeinfrastructure;
26 (e) Therelationshipbetweenworkforceneedsofnursinghomesandreimbursement;
27 (f) Theoverallsolvencyofthenursinghomesector;and
28 (g) The fairest means of addressing changes of ownership within reimbursement in a
29 mannerthatprotectsMedicaidprogramintegrity.
SB663-FN-A-ASAMENDEDBYTHESENATE
-Page2-
1 III. No later than December 31, 2026, the commissioner of the department of health and
2 human services, or their designee, shall submit to the speaker of the house of representatives, the
3 president of the senate, the house clerk, the senate clerk, the governor, the state library, and the
4 county-state finance commission any proposed changes to state law or regulations the department,
5 inconsultationwiththeworkinggroup,believesisnecessaryifthedepartmentintendstomodifythe
6 nursinghomeMedicaidratemethodology.
7 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
