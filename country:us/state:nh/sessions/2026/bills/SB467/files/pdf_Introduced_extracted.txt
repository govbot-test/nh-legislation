ANACT relative to the penalty for certain fentanyl-related offenses and establishing a

withdeathresulting.

ANALYSIS
Thisbill:
I. Addsmandatoryminimumsentencesforcertainfentanyl-relatedoffenses.
II. Sets a mandatory minimum sentence for the crime of distribution of a controlled drug with
deathresulting.
