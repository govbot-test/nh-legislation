1 1 NewSection;PossessionofFirearmsinaSafeSchoolZone. AmendRSA159byinsertingafter
2 section19-athefollowingnewsection:
3 159:19-b PossessionofaFirearminaSafeSchoolZone.
4 I. No person shall knowingly carry a firearm, as defined in RSA 173-B:1, XI, in a safe school
5 zone, as defined in RSA 193-D:1, II. Any person who violates the provisions of this paragraph shall
6 beguiltyofaclassAmisdemeanor.
7 II. Thissectionshallnotapplyto:
8 (a) Any person picking up or dropping off a student, provided the firearm remains in a
9 motorvehicle,isnotloaded,andisineitheralockedcontaineroralockedfirearmsrack.
10 (b) Any person authorized in writing by the school board or duly authorized designee to
11 possess a firearm. Such authorization shall specify the weapon or weapons that have been
12 authorized, the purpose and scope of the authorization, and the time period for which the
13 authorizationapplies.
14 (c) Any duly appointed law enforcement officer, properly trained school resource officer,
15 ormemberofthearmedservicesoftheUnitedStatesorNationalGuardwhenonduty.
16 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
