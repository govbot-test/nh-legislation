IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT eliminating the use of agitators in instances where temporary docks have been
removedfrombodiesofwater.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Navigation; Harbors; Coast Survey; Use of Aqua-Therm; Heating, Agitating, or Other Devices
2 inPublicWaters;SafetyHazard. AmendRSA270:33toreadasfollows:
3 270:33 Heating, Agitating, or Other Devices in Public Waters; Safety Hazard. No person shall
4 put, place, operate, or cause to be put, placed, or operated in the waters of this state any so-called
5 heating, agitating, or other device which inhibits or prevents the natural freezing of water, or
6 forming of ice, and thereby impedes either the ingress or egress to or from the ice from any property
7 other than that of the owner of the device. No person shall place or operate any
8 aforementioned device except as necessary to protect legally existing permanent structures
9 located adjacent to their shoreline from ice damage. [The] Any person or persons responsible
10 fortheplacementof [the] adeviceprotecting structuresby impeding ice formationshall ensure
11 that warning signs are posted to warn of its location. Said signs shall read DANGER, THIN ICE
12 andshallbeofsufficientsizetobereadableatadistanceofnotlessthan[150]200feet,andshallbe
13 visible from all directions and shall be equipped with reflectors and color-coded in a pattern unique
14 for this purpose only. The department of safety is hereby authorized to establish said unique design
15 and coloring and any homemade copies shall follow this design and coloring. The provisions of this
16 section shall be enforced by any law enforcement agency under the direction of the department of
17 safetypursuanttoRSA106-A:14andthedepartmentoffishandgamepursuanttoRSA206:26.
18 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
