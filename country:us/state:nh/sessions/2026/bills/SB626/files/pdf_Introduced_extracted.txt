SB626-FN-ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION
26-2114
07/09
626-FN
ANACT restricting right-to-know requests to persons domiciled or maintaining a
permanent residence in New Hampshire and requiring proof of domicile or
residencytofileright-to-knowrequests.
SPONSORS: Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Innis,
Dist7;Sen.McConkey,Dist3;Sen.Pearl,Dist17;Sen.Reardon,Dist15
COMMITTEE: Judiciary
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill restricts right-to-know requests to persons domiciled or maintaining a permanent
residence in New Hampshire and requires proof of domicile or residency to file right-to-know
requests.

26-2114
07/09
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT restricting right-to-know requests to persons domiciled or maintaining a
permanent residence in New Hampshire and requiring proof of domicile or
residencytofileright-to-knowrequests.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Paragraph; Public Officers and Employees; Access to Government Records and Meetings;
2 Definitions. AmendRSA91-A:1-abyinsertingafterparagraphIthefollowingnewparagraph:
3 I-a. “Citizen” means a natural person domiciled or who maintains a permanent place of
4 residenceinthisstate.
5 2 Public Officers and Employees; Access to Government Records and Meetings; Minutes and
6 RecordsAvailableforPublicInspection. AmendRSA91-A:4,IV(a)toreadasfollows:
7 (a) Each public body or agency shall, upon request by a citizen with sufficient
8 documentation to prove domicile or a permanent residence in this state, pursuant to RSA
9 654:12, I(c), for any governmental record reasonably described, make available for inspection and
10 copying any such governmental record within its files when such records are immediately available
11 for such release. News organizations that are located and operate in any state, country, or
12 territory except New Hampshire shall be exempt from the requirements of this
13 subparagraph.
14 3 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
