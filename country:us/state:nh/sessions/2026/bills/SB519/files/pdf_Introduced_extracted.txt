─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill establishes a civil cause of action for the use of unmanned aerial systems for certain
purposesthatviolateareasonableexpectationofprivacy,subjecttocertainexceptions.

SB519-FN-ASINTRODUCED
26-2075
09/08
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetotheuseofunmannedaerialsystems.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Chapter; Causes of Action Related to the Use of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems.
2 AmendRSAbyinsertingafterchapter507-Ithefollowingnewchapter:
3 CHAPTER507-J
4 CAUSESOFACTIONRELATEDTOTHEUSEOFSMALLUNMANNEDAIRCRAFTSYSTEMS
5 507-J:1 Definitions. Asusedinthischapter:
6 I. "Aircraft"means"aircraft"asitisdefinedinRSA422:3,VI.
7 II. "Imaging device" means any technology capable of recording or transmitting images,
8 sound,ordata.
9 III. "Operate"means"operateaircraft"asthattermisdefinedinRSA422:3,XXII.
10 IV. "Small unmanned aircraft" means "small unmanned aircraft" as it is defined in RSA
11 422:3,XXVII-a.
12 V. "Small unmanned aircraft system" means "small unmanned aircraft system" as it is
13 definedinRSA422:3,XXVII-b.
14 VI. "Surveillance" means the observation of one or more persons or property with enough
15 claritytogatheridentifyinginformation,habits,conduct,movements,oroccupancy.
16 507-J:2 ProhibitiononUsetoViolatePrivacy.
17 Neither the state, or any political subdivision thereof, nor any person, shall operate a small
18 unmanned aircraft system that includes an imaging device to conduct surveillance over private
19 property without written consent from the property owner, in any situation where a reasonable
20 expectation of privacy exists. For purposes of this section, a reasonable expectation of privacy shall
21 be presumed to exist in any location where an individual cannot be observed from a public ground-
22 levelposition.
23 507-J:3 Exceptions. RSA507-J:2shallnotapplyto:
24 I. Lawenforcementofficersoperatingpursuanttoavalidwarrant.
25 II. Law enforcement officers acting pursuant to exigent circumstances involving imminent
26 dangertolifeortheriskofdestructionofevidence.
27 III. Lawenforcementofficersperformingcounter-terrorismoperations.
28 IV. Regulatedutilityinspections.
29 V. Licensedbusinessoperations.
30 VI. Propertyappraisalfortaxes.
31 VII. Environmentalemergencyassessments.
SB519-FN-ASINTRODUCED
-Page2-
1 507-J:4Remedies.
2 I. A person alleging a violation of RSA 507-J:2 may bring an action for compensatory and
3 punitivedamagesandinjunctiverelief.
4 II. Anycivilremedyshallbeinadditiontoanyotherremedyavailableatlaw.
5 III. The court mayaward reasonable attorney's fees toapersonwho successfullyestablishes
6 aviolationofRSA507-J:2.
7 507-J:5 Severability.
8 If any provision of this chapter or the application thereof to any person or circumstances is held
9 invalid, such invalidity shall not affect other provisions or applications of the chapter which can be
10 given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this
11 chapteraredeclaredtobeseverable.
12 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
