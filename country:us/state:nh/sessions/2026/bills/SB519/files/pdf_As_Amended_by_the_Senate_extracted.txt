ANACT relativetotheuseofunmannedaerialsystems.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill establishes acivil cause of actionand acriminal penaltyfor theuse of unmanned aerial
systems for certain purposes that violate a reasonable expectation of privacy, subject to certain
exceptions.

ANACT relativetotheuseofunmannedaerialsystems.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Chapter; Causes of Action Related to the Use of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems.
2 AmendRSAbyinsertingafterchapter507-Ithefollowingnewchapter:
3 CHAPTER507-J
4 CAUSESOFACTIONRELATEDTOTHEUSEOFSMALLUNMANNEDAIRCRAFTSYSTEMS
5 507-J:1 Definitions. Asusedinthischapter:
6 I. "Aircraft"means"aircraft"asitisdefinedinRSA422:3,VI.
7 II. "Imaging device" means any technology capable of recording or transmitting images,
8 sound,ordata.
9 III. "Operate"means"operateaircraft"asthattermisdefinedinRSA422:3,XXII.
10 IV. "Small unmanned aircraft" means "small unmanned aircraft" as it is defined in RSA
11 422:3,XXVII-a.
12 V. "Small unmanned aircraft system" means "small unmanned aircraft system" as it is
13 definedinRSA422:3,XXVII-b.
14 VI.(a) “Surveillance”means:
15 (1) With respect to an owner, tenant, occupant, invitee, or licensee of privately
16 owned real property, the intentional observation of such persons with sufficient visual clarity to be
17 abletoobtaininformationabouttheiridentity,habits,conduct,movements,orwhereabouts;and
18 (2) Withrespecttoprivatelyownedrealproperty,theintentionalobservationofsuch
19 property’s physical improvements with sufficient visual clarity to be able to determine unique
20 identifyingfeaturesoritsoccupancybyoneormorepersons.
21 (b) "Surveillance" does not include the capture of photographs, electronic images, and
22 associated data by a drone operator that is operating for legitimate business purposes unrelated to
23 surveillanceinamannerconsistentwithapplicableFAArules,licenses,orexemptions.
24 507-J:2 ProhibitiononUsetoViolatePrivacy.
25 Neither the state, or any political subdivision thereof, nor any person, shall operate a small
26 unmanned aircraft system that includes an imaging device to conduct surveillance over private
27 property without written consent from the property owner, in any situation where a reasonable
28 expectation of privacy exists. For purposes of this section, a reasonable expectation of privacy shall
29 be presumed to exist in any location where a person cannot be observed from a public ground-level
30 position.
31 507-J:3 Exceptions. RSA507-J:2shallnotapplyto:

1 I. Lawenforcementofficers:
2 (a) Operatingpursuanttoavalidwarrant;
3 (b) Acting pursuant to exigent circumstances involving imminent danger to life or the
4 riskofdestructionofevidence;
5 (c) Countering a high risk of a terrorist attack by a specific individual or organization if
6 the United States Secretary of Homeland Security determines that credible intelligence indicates
7 thatthereissucharisk;
8 (d) Searchingforspecificallyidentifiedmissingpersons;
9 (e) Searchingforspecificallyidentifiedstolenvehicles;and
10 (f) Investigatingmotorvehicleaccidents.
11 (g) Performingotherlawenforcementduties.
12 II. InspectionsbyafiredepartmentinaccordancewiththeirresponsibilitiesoutlinedinRSA
13 154:2,III.
14 III. Inspections of critical infrastructure as defined in RSA 319-D:1, II, or non-utility-owned
15 electricity generators, by or on behalf of an owner or operator of a critical infrastructure facility for
16 thesolepurposeofmonitoring,inspecting,ormaintainingthefacility.
17 IV. Propertyappraisalfortaxes,providedthat:
18 (a) Each property owner shall be notified that the surveillance prohibited by RSA 507-
19 J:2 may occur and that the property owner may opt out of that surveillance without cost or other
20 consequence;
21 (b) Anyimagesofidentifiablepersonsshallbeblurredassoonaspracticable;and
22 (c) All surveillance images shall be permanently destroyed when they are no longer
23 neededforthepurposeforwhichtheywereobtained.
24 V. Environmental emergency assessments authorized by the department of environmental
25 services,providedthat:
26 (a) Anyimagesofidentifiablepersonsshallbeblurredassoonaspracticable;and
27 (b) All surveillance images shall be permanently destroyed when they are no longer
28 neededforthepurposeforwhichtheywereobtained.
29 VI. Anemployeeorcontractorofaninsurancecompanywhousesasmallunmannedaircraft
30 system solely for the purposes of underwriting, claims investigation, claims adjustment, assessment
31 ofpropertydamageorinvestigationofinsurancefraud.
32 VII. Railroad owners and easement holders, for the purpose of monitoring, inspecting, or
33 maintainingtherailroad.
34 507-J:4Remedies.
35 I. A person alleging a violation of RSA 507-J:2 may bring an action for compensatory and
36 punitivedamagesandinjunctiverelief.
37 II. Anycivilremedyshallbeinadditiontoanyotherremedyavailableatlaw.

1 III. The court mayaward reasonable attorney's fees toapersonwho successfullyestablishes
2 aviolationofRSA507-J:2.
3 507-J:5 Severability.
4 If any provision of this chapter or the application thereof to any person or circumstances is held
5 invalid, such invalidity shall not affect other provisions or applications of the chapter which can be
6 given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this
7 chapteraredeclaredtobeseverable.
8 2 New Subparagraphs; Unlawful Operation or Use of a Small Unmanned Aircraft System.
9 AmendRSA644:23,Ibyinsertingaftersubparagraph(d)thefollowingnewsubparagraphs:
10 (e) "Imaging device" means any technology capable of recording or transmitting images,
11 sound,ordata.
12 (f)(1) “Surveillance”means:
13 (A) With respect to an owner, tenant, occupant, invitee, or licensee of privately
14 owned real property, the intentional observation of such persons with sufficient visual clarity to be
15 abletoobtaininformationabouttheiridentity,habits,conduct,movements,orwhereabouts;and
16 (B) With respect to privately owned real property, the intentional observation of
17 such property’s physical improvements with sufficient visual clarity to be able to determine unique
18 identifyingfeaturesoritsoccupancybyoneormorepersons.
19 (2) "Surveillance" does not include the capture of photographs, electronic images,
20 and associated data by a drone operator that is operating for legitimate business purposes unrelated
21 tosurveillanceinamannerconsistentwithapplicableFAArules,licenses,orexemptions.
22 3 New Paragraph; Unlawful Operation or Use of Small Unmanned Aircraft System. Amend
23 RSA644:23byinsertingafterparagraphIVthefollowingnewparagraphs:
24 IV-a. A person is guilty of a class B felony if such person knowingly operates a small
25 unmanned aircraft system that includes an imaging device to conduct surveillance over private
26 property without written consent from the property owner, in any situation where a reasonable
27 expectation of privacy exists, in violation of RSA 507-J:2, and without being subject to an exception
28 listedinRSA507-J:3.
29 IV-b. A person is guilty of a class B felony if such person knowingly distributes or publishes
30 surveillanceobtainedinviolationofparagraphIV-a.
31 IV-c. ParagraphsIV-aandIV-bshallnotapplytoanyentityexemptunderparagraphIII.
32 4 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.

penalty for violation. Therefore, this bill may have an impact on the judicial and correctional
