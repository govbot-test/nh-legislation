AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill extends deadlines for disputingtowing and storagefees and requires invoices to include
information on how to challenge charges. This bill also revises the procedures and processes
concerning the maintenance of and utilization of a list of contracted tow companies used by the
divisionofstatepolicefortheremovalofvehicles.

AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill extends deadlines for disputingtowing and storagefees and requires invoices to include
information on how to challenge charges. This bill also revises the procedures and processes
concerning the maintenance of and utilization of a list of contracted tow companies used by the
divisionofstatepolicefortheremovalofvehicles.
