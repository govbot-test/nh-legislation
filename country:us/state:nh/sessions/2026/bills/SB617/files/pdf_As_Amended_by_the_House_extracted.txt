AMENDEDANALYSIS
Thisbill:
I. This bill substantially revises the procedures and processes concerning the maintenance of
and utilization of a list of contracted tow companies used by the division of state police for the
removalofvehicles.
II. Requires that invoices generated from towing and impounding vehicles clearly indicate the
process for appealing said tow or impoundment and extends the time period for a vehicle owner to
appealthereasonablenessoftowingandimpoundmentfeestothedepartmentofsafety.
III. Prohibits the division of motor vehicles from suspending a person's license or driving
privileges based on their failure to pay a debt related to a commercial entity's towing or storage of a
vehicle.

AMENDEDANALYSIS
Thisbill:
I. This bill substantially revises the procedures and processes concerning the maintenance of
and utilization of a list of contracted tow companies used by the division of state police for the
removalofvehicles.
II. Requires that invoices generated from towing and impounding vehicles clearly indicate the
process for appealing said tow or impoundment and extends the time period for a vehicle owner to
appealthereasonablenessoftowingandimpoundmentfeestothedepartmentofsafety.
III. Prohibits the division of motor vehicles from suspending a person's license or driving
privileges based on their failure to pay a debt related to a commercial entity's towing or storage of a
vehicle.
