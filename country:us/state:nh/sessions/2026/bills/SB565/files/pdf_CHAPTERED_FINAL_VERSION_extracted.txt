ANACT requiring the commissioner of the insurance department to prepare and publish a

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This bill requires the commissioner of the insurance department to prepare and publish a report
regardingfortifiedhomeandcommercialstandards,andothermitigationandresiliencyprograms.

STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT requiring the commissioner of the insurance department to prepare and publish a
report regarding fortified home and commercial standards, and other mitigation and
resiliencyprograms.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 202:1 New Subdivision; Reporting. Amend RSA 412 by inserting after section 14 the following new
2 subdivision:
3 Reporting
4 412:14-a Reporting.
5 I. To promote awareness of and incentives for fortified homes and commercial property
6 standards,andothermitigationandresiliencyprograms,thecommissionershallutilize,develop,orcause
7 to be developed a report which shall be provided on a readily available basis to purchasers of personal
8 property and commercial property risks insurance related to fortified home and commercial standards,
9 and other mitigation and resiliency programs. The information may include references to available
10 information on areas that may be at risk of price increases due to extreme precipitation, storm surge,
11 coastalflooding,highwinds,hurricanes,andhail. Forthepurposesofthissection,thecommissionermay
12 consultRSA483-B:22,FEMAfloodmaps,theNationalFloodInsuranceProgram,andanyotheravailable
13 mapping that may include projected threats, including first street maps, the New Hampshire coastal
14 viewer,andthedepartmentofenvironmentalservicescoastalprogram,todetermineareasofpresentand
15 futurerisktoinsurancecoverage. Unlessprohibitedbylaw,thereportmayalsoincludeasummaryofany
16 information the commissioner may have received, provided by insurers, on the rate of premium cost
17 increases in such areas. This report shall be made available by January 1, 2028, and in subsequent 5-
18 year intervals. The commissioner may establish an advisory council to undertake these duties, under
19 RSA 400-A:11, which may be authorized to seek grants, donations, and other sources of funding, or fees
20 authorizedunderRSA400:A-29,XVI.
21 II. For the purposes of this section, "fortified homes and commercial property standards, and
22 other mitigation and resiliency programs" means measures taken to reduce loss due to extreme weather
23 eventssuchasprecipitationevents,stormsurge,andcoastalflooding,highwinds,hurricanes,orhail.
24 III. Thecommissioner shall publishthereport onthe insurancedepartment'swebsiteand ensure
25 thatthereportisreadilyavailabletoconsumersinNewHampshire.
26 202:2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
Approved:June19,2026
EffectiveDate:January01,2027
