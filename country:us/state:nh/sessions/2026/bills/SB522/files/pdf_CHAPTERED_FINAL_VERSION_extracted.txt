ANACT re-establishingthecommissiontostudytheeconomicimpactoftheartsandculturein
NewHampshire.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 65:1 New Subdivision; Commission to Study the Economic Impact of the Arts and Culture in New
2 Hampshire. AmendRSA19-Abyinsertingaftersection13thefollowingnewsubdivision:
3 CommissiontoStudytheEconomicImpactoftheArtsandCultureinNewHampshire
4 19-A:14 CommissionEstablished.
5 I. ThereisestablishedacommissiontostudytheeconomicimpactoftheartsandcultureinNew
6 Hampshire.
7 II. Thecommissionshallconsistofthefollowingmembers:
8 (a) Three members of the house of representatives, one of whom shall be a member of the
9 commerce and consumer affairs committee and one of whom shall be a member of the finance
10 committee,appointedbythespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives.
11 (b) Onememberofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
12 (c) Onememberofthestatecouncilonthearts,appointedbythecouncil.
13 (d) Thecommissionerofthedepartmentofbusinessandeconomicaffairs,ordesignee.
14 (e) One member each from the division of the arts, the division of libraries, the division of
15 historical resources, and the New Hampshire film and television commission, appointed by the
16 commissionerofthedepartmentofculturalresources.
17 (f) One member from the board of directors of Arts4NH, appointed by the president of the
18 organization.
19 (g) One member from the board of directors of the New Hampshire Theatre Alliance,
20 appointedbythatorganization.
21 (h) One member from the board of trustees of the League of New Hampshire Craftsmen,
22 appointedbythepresidentoftheboardoftrusteesoftheorganization.
23 (i) One member from the board of trustees of the White Mountains Attractions Association,
24 appointedbythatorganization.
25 (j) Onerepresentativeoftheperformingarts,appointedbythegovernor.
26 (k) OnerepresentativeofNewHampshireartmuseums,appointedbythegovernor.
27 (l) OnerepresentativeofNewHampshirehistorymuseums,appointedbythegovernor.
28 III. Legislative members of the commission shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when
29 attendingtothedutiesofthecommission.
30 IV. The commission shall study the economic impact of the arts and culture in New Hampshire
31 and recommend ways in which the state can enhance and expand this sector of the New Hampshire

1 economy. In the performance of its duties, the commission may solicit information and testimony from
2 localartistsandanygroupororganization,andotherstateswithinformationrelevanttothestudy.
3 V. Themembersofthecommissionshallelectachairpersonfromamongthemembers. Thefirst
4 meeting of the commission shall be called by the first-named house member. The first meeting of the
5 commission shall be held within 45 days of the effective date of this section. Nine members of the
6 commissionshallconstituteaquorum.
7 VI. Thecommissionshallreportitsfindingsandanyrecommendationsforproposedlegislationto
8 thespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives,thepresidentofthesenate,thehouseclerk,thesenateclerk,
9 thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
10 65:2 Prospective Repeal. RSA 19-A:14, relative to the commission to study the economic impact of

13 I. Section2ofthisactshalltakeeffectNovember1,2026.
II. Theremainderofthisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
