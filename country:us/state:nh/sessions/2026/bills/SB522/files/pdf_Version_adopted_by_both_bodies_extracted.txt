IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT re-establishing the commission to study the economic impact of the arts and
cultureinNewHampshire.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Subdivision; Commission to Study the Economic Impact of the Arts and Culture in New
2 Hampshire. AmendRSA19-Abyinsertingaftersection13thefollowingnewsubdivision:
3 CommissiontoStudytheEconomicImpactoftheArtsandCultureinNewHampshire
4 19-A:14 CommissionEstablished.
5 I. There is established a commission to study the economic impact of the arts and culture in
6 NewHampshire.
7 II. Thecommissionshallconsistofthefollowingmembers:
8 (a) Three members of the house of representatives, one of whom shall be a member of
9 the commerce and consumer affairs committee and one of whom shall be a member of the finance
10 committee,appointedbythespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives.
11 (b) Onememberofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
12 (c) Onememberofthestatecouncilonthearts,appointedbythecouncil.
13 (d) Thecommissionerofthedepartmentofbusinessandeconomicaffairs,ordesignee.
14 (e) One member each from the division of the arts, the division of libraries, the division
15 of historical resources, and the New Hampshire film and television commission, appointed by the
16 commissionerofthedepartmentofculturalresources.
17 (f) One member from the board of directors of Arts4NH, appointed by the president of
18 theorganization.
19 (g) One member from the board of directors of the New Hampshire Theatre Alliance,
20 appointedbythatorganization.
21 (h) One member from the board of trustees of the League of New Hampshire craftsmen,
22 appointedbythepresidentoftheboardoftrusteesoftheorganization.
23 (i) One member from the board of trustees of the White Mountains Attractions
24 Association,appointedbythatorganization.
25 (j) Onerepresentativeoftheperformingarts,appointedbythegovernor.
26 (k) OnerepresentativeofNewHampshireartmuseums,appointedbythegovernor.
27 (l) OnerepresentativeofNewHampshirehistorymuseums,appointedbythegovernor.
28 III. Legislativemembersofthecommissionshallreceivemileageatthelegislativeratewhen
29 attendingtothedutiesofthecommission.
SB522-VERSIONADOPTEDBYBOTHBODIES
-Page2-
1 IV. The commission shall study the economic impact of the arts and culture in New
2 Hampshire and recommend ways in which the state can enhance and expand this sector of the New
3 Hampshire economy. In the performance of its duties, the commission may solicit information and
4 testimony from local artists and any group or organization, and other states with information
5 relevanttothestudy.
6 V. The members of the commission shall elect a chairperson from among the members. The
7 first meeting of the commission shall be called by the first-named house member. The first meeting
8 of the commission shall be held within 45 days of the effective date of this section. Nine members of
9 thecommissionshallconstituteaquorum.
10 VI. The commission shall report its findings and any recommendations for proposed
11 legislation to the speaker of the house of representatives, the president of the senate, the house
12 clerk,thesenateclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
13 2 Prospective Repeal. RSA 19-A:14, relative to the commission to study the economic impact of
14 theartsandcultureinNewHampshire,isrepealed.
15 3 EffectiveDate.
16 I. Section2ofthisactshalltakeeffectNovember1,2026.
17 II. Theremainderofthisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
