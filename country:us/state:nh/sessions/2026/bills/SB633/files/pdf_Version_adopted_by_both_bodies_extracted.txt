STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetodonationsreceivedbythegranitepatronoftheartsfund.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Council on the Arts; State Art Fund; Granite Patron of the Arts Fund. Amend RSA 19-A:9, II
2 toreadasfollows:
3 II. The division and the council are authorized to institute programs to solicit and receive
4 any gifts, grants, donations, or to receive federal matching funds made for the encouragement of the
5 arts and to deposit such gifts, grants, or donations in the New Hampshire council on the arts fund
6 under this section. The division and the council shall acknowledge receipt of any gifts, grants, or
7 donations within 15 days of receipt on a form provided by the commissioner of the department of
8 revenue administration. Donations greater than or equal to $50,000 shall require the
9 approval of the governor and executive council. Expenditures greater than or equal to
10 $50,000, and expenditures on employees of the division, shall require the approval of the
11 governor and executive council, and with a report to the fiscal committee of the general
12 court.
13 III. Quarterly expenditure reports shall be filed with the governor, executive
14 council,andthefiscalcommitteeofthegeneralcourt.
15 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
