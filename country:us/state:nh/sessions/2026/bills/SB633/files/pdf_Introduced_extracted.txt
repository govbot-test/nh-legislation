SB633-FN-ASINTRODUCED
26-2187
07/05
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishing a voluntary surcharge program and fund for the division of the arts
andstatecouncilonthearts.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Subdivision; The State and Its Government; Council on the Arts; Performance Arts
2 Ticket Surcharge Program. Amend RSA 19-A by inserting after section 15 the following new
3 subdivision:
4 PerformanceArtsTicketSurchargeProgram
5 19-A:16 PerformanceArtsTicketSurchargeProgram.
6 I. For purposes of this section, "performing arts venue" means a facility primarily dedicated
7 tothepresentationoftheperformingarts.
8 II. There is hereby established a voluntary surcharge program for New Hampshire
9 performing arts venues, with a capacity between 100 and 2,500 persons and with ticket sellers at
10 such venues who charge no more than $0.50 per ticket, to fund the division of the arts and the New
11 Hampshire state council on the arts. The surcharge shall be administered by the department of
12 natural and cultural resources and deposited into the performance art voluntary ticket surcharge
13 fund created in paragraph III. The division of the arts shall be authorized, upon the approval of the
14 commissioner of natural and cultural resources, or the approval of the governor and executive
15 council,tomakegrants,orprovidematchinggrants,tothefund.
16 III. There is hereby established in the department of natural and cultural resources a
17 performance art voluntary ticket surcharge fund, which shall be kept distinct and separate from all
18 other funds. All moneys in the fund shall be nonlapsing. All moneys and interest in the
19 performance art voluntary ticket surcharge fund shall be used for the purpose of supporting the
20 administration, functions, and grants, including matching grants, of the division of the arts and the
21 NewHampshirestatecouncilontheartsandshallnotbeusedforanyotherpurpose.
22 IV. TheNewHampshirestatecouncilfortheartsandthedivisionoftheartsshalldevelopa
23 memorandum of understanding, approved by the commissioner of natural and cultural resources, to
24 governvoluntaryparticipationintheperformanceartsticketsurchargeprogram.
25 V. The commissioner of natural and cultural resources shall adopt rules, under RSA 541-A,
26 regarding the establishment of the surcharge voluntary program, a memorandum of understanding,
27 thedesignationofthesurchargeasacharitabledonation,andexpendituresfromthefund.
28 2 New Subparagraph; The State and Its Government; State Treasurer; Application of Receipts.
29 AmendRSA6:12,I(b)byinsertingaftersubparagraph(410)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
SB633-FN-ASINTRODUCED
-Page2-
1 (411) Moneys deposited into the performance art ticket surcharge fund established
2 inRSA19-A:16,III.
3 3 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
