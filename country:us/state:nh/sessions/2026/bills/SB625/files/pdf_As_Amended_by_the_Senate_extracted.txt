07/09
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishing a committee to study options for family members of intentional
homicidevictimswherethedepartmentofjusticedoesnotfilechargesinacase.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Committee Established. There is established a committee to study options for information for
2 familymembersofintentionalhomicidevictimswherethedepartmentofjusticedoesnotfilecharges
3 inacase.
4 2 MembershipandCompensation.
5 I. Themembersofthecommitteeshallbeasfollows:
6 (a) Twomembersofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
7 (b) Three members of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the
8 houseofrepresentatives.
9 II. Legislative members of the committee shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when
10 attendingtothedutiesofthecommittee.
11 3 Duties. The committee shall study options for information for family members of intentional
12 homicide victims where the department of justice does not file charges in a case. The committee
13 shall:
14 I. Reviewtherelevantlawsofotherstates;
15 II. Determine whether the New Hampshire constitution would allow for relevant laws in
16 otherstatestobeimplementedinNewHampshire;
17 III. Review the current process for keeping family members of intentional homicide victims
18 updatedonthecaseanddetermininghowtoaddressanygapsfoundinthatcommunication;
19 IV. Investigate whether there are any systemic problems with the investigatory process and
20 ifanyarefound,determininghowthoseproblemscouldbeaddressed;and
21 V. Investigate the feasibility of extending the statute of limitations for crimes relevant to
22 thisstudy.
23 4 Chairperson; Quorum. The members of the study committee shall elect a chairperson from
24 among the members. The first meeting of the committee shall be called by the first-named senate
25 member. The first meeting of thecommitteeshall be held within45days of theeffectivedateof this
26 section. Threemembersofthecommitteeshallconstituteaquorum.
27 5 Report. The committee shall report its findings and any recommendations for proposed
28 legislation to the president of the senate, the speaker of the house of representatives, the senate
29 clerk,thehouseclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
30 6 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
