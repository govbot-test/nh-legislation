26-2060

STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT increasingthelimitfortransfersofappropriationsinCarrollCounty.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Carroll County Appropriation Transfer Limit; Increase. Amend RSA 24:13-d, I to read as
2 follows:
3 I. Prior to the transfer of an appropriation, or any part thereof exceeding [$1,000] $10,000,
4 or an accumulative total exceeding [$1,000] $10,000, from one line item to another, the following
5 procedure shall apply: a request to transfer an appropriation shall be made in writing, by the
6 department head, whether elected or appointed, to the county commissioners, who, if they approve
7 thesame,shallreportinwritingtheirrecommendationtotheexecutivecommittee.
8 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
