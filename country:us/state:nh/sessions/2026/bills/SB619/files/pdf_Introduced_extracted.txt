ANACT establishingproceduresforexpeditedcourthearingsanddispositionofconfiscated

SPONSORS: Sen. Abbas, Dist 22; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Sullivan, Dist 18; Sen. McGough,
Dist 11; Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. Murphy, Dist 16; Sen. Innis, Dist 7; Sen.
Birdsell,Dist19;Rep.AlexanderJr.,Hills.29;Rep.C.Harvey,Ches.6
COMMITTEE: Judiciary
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill establishes procedures for expedited court hearings and disposition of animals
confiscatedduetoallegationsofanimalcruelty.

SB619-FN-ASINTRODUCED
26-2164
08/06
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishingproceduresforexpeditedcourthearingsanddispositionofconfiscated
animals.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 CrueltytoAnimals;Court HearingUponConfiscationof Animals. AmendRSA644:8, IV(a)(3)
2 toreadasfollows:
3 (3) Courts shall give cases in which animals have been confiscated by an arresting
4 officer priority on the court calendar. In cases in which animals have been confiscated by an
5 arresting officer or his or her agency, a status hearing shall be held by the court within 14 days of
6 theconfiscationoftheanimals.
7 (A) The scope of the hearing shall be limited to whether the impounding
8 of the animal or animals was authorized. Upon such a showing, the court may require the
9 defendant or appellant to post a bond or other security in an amount not exceeding $1,000
10 for each animal in custody or make a determination of disposition that is in the best
11 interestoftheanimal'shealth,safety,andwellbeing. Iftheconfiscatedanimalisco-owned
12 by the defendant, the court may transfer the defendant's interest in the property to the
13 remaining owner or co-owners equitably if the court determines that such is in the best
14 interestoftheanimal'shealth,safety,andwellbeing.
15 (B) Such bond or security shall be posted to the court within 30 days. If
16 such bond or security is not paid within 30 days after the court orders the bond or security
17 to be posted, the animals shall be forfeited to the arresting officer, the arresting officer's
18 agency, or the arresting officer's agency's designee. The court may, for good cause, extend
19 the deadline by no more than 15 days. A court shall order the return of any bond or
20 securityuponacourtapprovedagreementofthepartiesorafindingofnotguilty.
21 (C) Neither the result of a hearing provided for under this paragraph
22 nor a statement of an owner made at any such hearing shall be admissible in any criminal
23 prosecutionrelatedtotheimpoundmentoftheanimaloranimals.
24 (D) Failure of the owner to appear in court without prior notice, or
25 without proof of emergent circumstances within 3 days shall not prevent the court from
26 conductingthehearingormakingadetermination.
27 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
