2 RSA21-P:15-c,IIItoreadasfollows:
3 III. To obtain a state records check only, the department shall submit a state criminal
4 history records release form to the division of state police. To obtain both the federal and the state
5 records check, the department shall submit the criminal history records release form and applicant
6 fingerprint card to the division of state police, which shall conduct a criminal history records check
7 through its records and through the Federal Bureau of Investigation. Upon completion of the
8 background investigation, the division of state police shall report any criminal conviction
9 information to the commissioner or to a department of safety employee as designated by the
10 commissioner who is authorized to receive criminal conviction information. The
11 department may submit fingerprint information electronically, in accordance with procedures
12 established by the division of state police. The department shall maintain the confidentiality and
13 securityofallcriminalhistoryrecordsinformationreceivedpursuanttothisparagraph.
14 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
