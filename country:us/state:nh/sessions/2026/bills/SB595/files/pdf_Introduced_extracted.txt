ANACT relative to the establishment of a remediation pathway for water systems
requiredtoundergofrequenttesting.
SPONSORS: Sen. Murphy, Dist 16; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Pearl,
Dist17;Sen.PerkinsKwoka,Dist21
COMMITTEE: EnergyandNaturalResources
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This enables a community water system that is sanctioned by department of environmental
services for missing a required water test, or submitting late water test results to return to a
standardscheduleoftestingundercertainconditions.

6 uponsubmitting6consecutivewatertestsinatimelyfashion.
