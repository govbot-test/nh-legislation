ANACT relativetorulemakingfortransientnon-communitywatersystems.

AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill requires rules made by the department of environmental services relative to drinking
water include an option for sampling of transient non-community water systems under certain
circumstances.

IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetorulemakingfortransientnon-communitywatersystems.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 DrinkingWaterRules. AmendRSA485:3,I(c)toreadasfollows:
2 (c) Criteria and procedures to assure compliance with the levels or methods determined
3 under subparagraph (b), including quality control monitoring and testing procedures and standards
4 to ensure compliance with such levels or methods; criteria and standards to ensure proper operation
5 and maintenance of the system; requirements as to the minimum quality of water which may be
6 delivered to the consumer; and requirements with respect to siting new facilities. Such rules shall
7 be no less stringent than the most recent national Primary Drinking Water Regulations in effect, as
8 issued or promulgated by the United States Environmental Protection Agency. Such rules shall
9 include the option for transient non-community water systems to remain on quarterly
10 sampling if a sample is missed consistent with the criteria and procedures in 40 C.F.R.
11 section141.854etseq,asamendedfromtimetotime.
12 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
