ANACT relative to the detention of a minor attaining the age of 18 during the pendency of a
delinquencyaction.
SPONSORS: Sen.Sullivan,Dist18;Rep.Rice,Hills.38
COMMITTEE: ChildrenandFamilyLaw
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill modifies the facility in which alleged delinquent minors may be held in detention pending an
adjudicatory hearing, permitting alleged delinquent minors to be housed in a county corrections facility
oncereachingage18.
