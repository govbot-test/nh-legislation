STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT including a determination of the best interest of the animal in the definition of
fosterhome.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Paragraph; Foster Home; Definition. Amend RSA 437:1 by inserting after paragraph V
2 thefollowingnewparagraph:
3 VI. "Foster home" means a private residence in New Hampshire associated with and
4 inspected by an animal shelter facility used to hold animals when it is deemed by a licensed
5 veterinarian to be in the best interest of the animal's health, safety, and wellbeing including
6 pregnantorlactatinganimals.
7 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
