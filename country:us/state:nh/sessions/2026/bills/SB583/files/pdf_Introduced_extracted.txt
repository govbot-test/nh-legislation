andreportingsystemandmakesanappropriationtherefor.

26-2224
07/06
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT directing the department of education to create an education funding
transparencydataandreportingsystem.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Section; Education; The State School Organization; Funding Data and Transparency
2 ReportingSystem. AmendRSA186byinsertingaftersection77thefollowingnewsection:
3 186:77-a FundingDataandTransparencyReportingSystem.
4 I. The department of education shall create an education funding data and transparency
5 reporting system (system). The system shall include a longitudinal data system incorporating, at a
6 minimum, data on basic education funding allocations, disbursements, and expenditures over the
7 past 10 years disaggregated at the intermediate unit, district, and school levels. The department
8 shall create data visualization dashboards to simulate changes to the funding formula, including
9 changing weights and measures of funding allocations and distributions of funds based on school
10 characteristics. Data visualization dashboards shall be accessible to the appropriate staff at state
11 entities,includingthelegislatureandgovernor'soffice.
12 II. Theeducationfundingdataandtransparencyreportingsystemshall:
13 (a) Be agnostic regarding data type and capable of accessing and integrating any data
14 typefromanydatasystemacrossparticipatingfeedersystemsfrompartnerentities;
15 (b) Have advanced data management capabilities to clean and prepare data, create
16 masterrecords,andflaganyerrorsormissingdata;
17 (c) Have automated data governance capabilities to audit and track the lineage of data
18 editsandchangesmadebydifferentauthorizedusers;
19 (d) Have advanced analytics functionality, including data mining and machine learning
20 capabilities to analyze the longitudinal data for program evaluation, predictive analytics for
21 forecasting and projections into the future, and natural language processing for allowing partner
22 entities to analyze large volumes of text found in surveys, grants, accreditation reports, and other
23 text-heavydocuments;
24 (e) Have business intelligence capabilities for partner entities to easily generate
25 reportingandvisualizationsthroughalowcodeornocodedraganddropinterface;
26 (f) Include public-facing dashboards that only include aggregate-level data that is
27 appropriateforthegeneralpublictoview;
28 (g) Include restricted dashboards and environments for partner entities to view data
29 thatissubjecttotheprotectionsof20U.S.C.§1232,withrole-basedpermissions;
SB583-FN-ASINTRODUCED
-Page2-
1 (h) Support data exchanges and interoperability with the department of education,
2 general court, governor's office, and other existing data systems in this state to be identified by the
3 department;and
4 (i) Ensure routine and ongoing compliance with 20 U.S.C. § 1232g and other relevant
5 privacylawsandpoliciesby:
6 (1) Usingde-identifieddataindataresearchandpublicreporting;
7 (2) Implementingestablishedpoliciesregardingsmallcellsuppression;
8 (3) Disposingofinformationthatisnolongerneeded;
9 (4) Providingdatasecurity,includingthecapacityforaudittrails;
10 (5) Performing regular audits for compliance with data privacy and security
11 standards;and
12 (6) Implementing guidelines and policies that prevent the reporting of any
13 potentiallyidentifiabledata.
14 III. The sum of $500,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, and for every year
15 thereafter, is hereby appropriated to the department of education for the purpose of operating and
16 maintaining an education funding transparency data and reporting system. These funds shall be
17 nonlapsing. The governor is authorized to draw a warrant for said sums out of any money in the
18 treasurynototherwiseappropriated.
19 2 Appropriation; Funding Data and Transparency Reporting System. The sum of $900,000 for
20 the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027 is hereby appropriated to the department of education for the
21 purpose of creating an education funding transparency data and reporting system. These funds
22 shall be nonlapsing. The governor is authorized to draw a warrant for said sums out of any money
23 inthetreasurynototherwiseappropriated.
24 3 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
