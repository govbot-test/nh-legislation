ANACT allowing the director of the division of motor vehicles to adopt administrative
rulesregardingtheuseofelectronicsignatures.
SPONSORS: Sen. Lang, Dist 2; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Sen. Birdsell,
Dist 19; Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Innis, Dist 7; Sen.
Altschiller, Dist 24; Sen. Gray, Dist 6; Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. McConkey, Dist
3; Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. Prentiss, Dist 5; Sen. Murphy, Dist 16; Sen. Long,
Dist 20; Sen. Carson, Dist 14; Sen. Rochefort, Dist 1; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen.
Ricciardi, Dist 9; Rep. Moffett, Merr. 4; Rep. S. Smith, Sull. 3; Rep. Maggiore,
Rock.23
COMMITTEE: Transportation
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
Thisbillauthorizesthedirectorofthedivisionofmotorvehiclestoadoptrulesregardingrecords,
documents, applications, and forms that can be signed electronically, and requires electronic
signaturestobeacceptedonsuchforms.

09/08
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT allowing the director of the division of motor vehicles to adopt administrative
rulesregardingtheuseofelectronicsignatures.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 ShortTitle. Thisbillmaybeknownasandcitedtoasthe"DMVe-SignatureAct."
2 2 New Section; Words and Phrases Defined; Signature. Amend RSA 259 by inserting after
3 section100-athefollowingnewsection:
4 259:100-aa Signature. “Signature” means a written signature, or an electronic signature as
5 definedinRSA294-E:2andwhenauthorizedbythedirector.
6 3 Administration of Motor Vehicle Laws; Rulemaking Authority. Amend RSA 260:5 to read as
7 follows:
8 260:5 Rulemaking Authority. All rulemaking authority expressly granted under the provisions
9 of this title to the department or any of its administrators, officials, divisions or bureaus, or to any
10 agency as defined in RSA 541-A:1, II, shall be subject to the provisions of RSA 541-A. The director
11 shall accept electronic signatures as defined in RSA 294-E:2, VIII, on such records,
12 documents, applications, or forms as the director may prescribe, by rule, pursuant to RSA
13 541-A. Whenever, under the provisions of this title, the department or any of its administrators,
14 officials, divisions or bureaus, or any other agency, has been granted authority to prescribe or
15 otherwise require forms, reports or other matter included in the definition of rule in RSA 541-A:1,
16 XV, such authority shall be construed as rulemaking authority and shall be subject to RSA 541-A.
17 Provided, however, that under the provisions of this title wherever any administrator, official,
18 division or bureau in the department has been granted the authority to adopt rules, either expressly
19 or as stated above, such authority shall be subject to the approval of the commissioner. Unless
20 otherwise specified in this title, appeals from any order or decision of the director, commissioner,
21 division, department or other agency acting under the provisions of this title shall be pursued as
22 providedinRSA541.
23 4 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
