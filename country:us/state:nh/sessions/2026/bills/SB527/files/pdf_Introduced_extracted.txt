ANACT relative to written notice provided by state agencies to municipalities regarding

SPONSORS: Sen.Lang,Dist2;Sen.Pearl,Dist17;Sen.Watters,Dist4;Sen.Sullivan,Dist18;

ANALYSIS
This bill broadens the scope of what comprises notice from first class mail to all written forms of
notice.

3 ensure written notice to and afford all affected municipalities reasonable opportunity to submit
4 data, views, or comments with respect to the issuance of a permit, license, or any action within its

8 theprovisionofadditionalmunicipalservices.

10 thetownorcityclerk.
11 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
