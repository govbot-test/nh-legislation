SB655-ASINTRODUCED
26-3263
04/05
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to employee leasing companies and workers' compensation coverage
options.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Employee Leasing Companies; License Application and Fees. Amend the introductory
2 paragraphofRSA277-B:5,IVtoreadasfollows:
3 IV. Every application for an original, renewal, or restricted license[,] shall be accompanied
4 by evidence satisfactory to the commissioner that the leased employees are covered by workers'
5 compensation insurance issued by a carrier admitted to write such coverage in this state for leased
6 employees for whom the employee leasing company has the contractual obligation to
7 maintain workers compensation coverage. No unlicensed leasing company shall be provided
8 workers' compensation coverage. An employee leasing company, if it has the contractual
9 obligation to maintain workers' compensation coverage for leased employees, may meet its
10 obligation to provide such insurance for leased employees through no more than 2 policies. When 2
11 policies are issued, one shall be issued in the voluntary market, and the other shall be issued in the
12 residualmarket.
13 2 New Paragraph; Employee Leasing Deemed Co-Employment. Amend RSA 277-B:9 by
14 insertingafterparagraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
15 II-a. Either the employee leasing company or the client company shall purchase and
16 maintainworkers'compensationinsuranceforthoseemployeesinanemployeeleasingarrangement.
17 3 Employee Leasing Companies; Exclusivity and Vicarious Liability. Amend RSA 277-B:10 to
18 readasfollows:
19 277-B:10 Exclusivity and Vicarious Liability. An employee leasing company and its client
20 companyshall both be entitled to the exclusivity of remedy provisions of RSA 281-A:8regardless of
21 which entity purchases the workers' compensation insurance, and the employee leasing
22 company shall not be vicariously liable for the actions or omissions of the client company and the
23 client company shall not be vicariously liable for the actions or omissions of the employee leasing
24 company. Nothing in this section shall prohibit any direct contractual liability between the
25 employee leasing company and the client company, nor shall the same limit any liability or
26 responsibilityimposedbythischapter.
27 4 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
