ANACT establishing a task force to assess the development of housing at Great Bay
communitycollegeandauthorizingthecollegetheright tousevacantpropertyfor
thepurposeofdevelopinghousing.
SPONSORS: Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Innis, Dist 7; Sen. Lang, Dist 2;
Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Reardon, Dist 15; Sen.
Perkins Kwoka, Dist 21; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. Prentiss,
Dist 5; Sen. Altschiller, Dist 24; Rep. Mandelbaum, Rock. 21; Rep. Moffett, Merr.
4;Rep.Meuse,Rock.37;Rep.Cornell,Hills.22
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
The bill establishes a task force to study housing feasibility, funding mechanisms, operational
models, and resident support systems. This bill also authorizes the development of housing on the
Great Bay community college property within the Pease development area, in coordination with
relevantstateagencies.

IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishing a task force to assess the development of housing at Great Bay
communitycollegeandauthorizingthecollegetheright tousevacantpropertyfor
thepurposeofdevelopinghousing.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Section; Great Bay Community College Housing Development. Amend RSA 12-G by
2 insertingaftersection13thefollowingnewsection:
3 12-G:13-a GreatBayCommunityCollegeHousingDevelopment;TaskForce.
4 I. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, including RSA 188-F and RSA 674, the
5 authority may, in consultation with the community college system of New Hampshire and the
6 department of military affairs and veterans services, authorize, lease, or otherwise facilitate the
7 construction and operation of housing on the Great Bay community college property within the
8 Pease development area. Such housing may be designed to accommodate Great Bay community
9 collegestudents, faculty, and staff, and active or reserve military personnel assigned to duties in the
10 NewHampshirenationalguard.
11 II. Notwithstanding RSA 14:49, there is established a task force on housing at Great Bay
12 communitycollegetostudyandmakerecommendationsconcerningthefeasibilityofdevelopingsuch
13 housingfacilities. Thetaskforceshall:
14 (a) Review the permissibility of military and student housing on Pease Development
15 Authority property pursuant to federal and state law, as well as applicable property conveyance
16 documents and agreements with federal agencies, and review possible avenues for amending such
17 laws, conveyance documents, and agreements to make such military and student housing
18 permissible.
19 (b) Consider community college system residence hall models, leased apartment models,
20 andmilitarydormitoryorhousingmodels.
21 (c) Review potential construction and operation funding sources, including subsidies,
22 grants,low-incomehousingprograms,orstateorfederalappropriations.
23 (d) Determine ownership and leasing models, construction financing mechanisms, and
24 revenuesourcessufficienttooperateandmaintainthefacility.
25 (e) Evaluatestaffingoptionsforresidentiallife,maintenance,andsecurity.
26 (f) Compile data on cost, occupancy, resident demographics, student outcomes,
27 affordabilityandsustainability.
28 III. Thetaskforceshallincludethefollowingmembers:
SB490-VERSIONADOPTEDBYBOTHBODIES
-Page2-
1 (a) Onesenatorappointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
2 (b) Onerepresentativeappointedbythespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives.
3 (c) The commissioner of the department of military affairs and veterans services, or
4 designee.
5 (d) ThechancellorofthecommunitycollegesystemofNewHampshire,ordesignee.
6 (e) TheexecutivedirectorofthePeasedevelopmentauthority,ordesignee.
7 (f) ThepresidentofGreatBaycommunitycollege,ordesignee.
8 IV. The task force shall submit a report with findings and any recommendations for
9 proposed legislation to the governor, the president of the senate, the speaker of the house of
10 representatives,andthecityofPortsmouthnolaterthanDecember30,2026.
11 V. Legislative members of the task force shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when
12 attendingtothedutiesofthetaskforce.
13 2 Repeal. RSA 12-G:13-a, relative to the Great Bay Community College Housing Development
14 taskforce,isrepealed.
15 3 EffectiveDate.
16 I. Section2ofthisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2029.
17 II. Theremainderofthisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
