2026SESSION
26-2055
06/08
490
ANACT establishing a task force to assess the development of housing at Great Bay
communitycollegeandauthorizingthecollegetheright tousevacantpropertyfor
thepurposeofdevelopinghousing.
SPONSORS: Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Innis, Dist 7; Sen. Lang, Dist 2;
Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Reardon, Dist 15; Sen.
Perkins Kwoka, Dist 21; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. Prentiss,
Dist 5; Sen. Altschiller, Dist 24; Rep. Mandelbaum, Rock. 21; Rep. Moffett, Merr.
4;Rep.Meuse,Rock.37;Rep.Cornell,Hills.22
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
The bill establishes a task force to study housing feasibility, funding mechanisms, operational
models, and resident support systems. This bill also authorizes the development of housing on the
Great Bay community college property within the Pease development area, in coordination with
relevantstateagencies.

SB490-ASINTRODUCED
26-2055
06/08
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishing a task force to assess the development of housing at Great Bay
communitycollegeandauthorizingthecollegetheright tousevacantpropertyfor
thepurposeofdevelopinghousing.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Subdivision; Great Bay Community College Housing Development. Amend RSA 12-G by
2 insertingaftersection13thefollowingnewsubdivision:
3 GreatBayCommunityCollegeHousingDevelopment
4 12-G:13-a GreatBayCommunityCollegeHousingDevelopment;TaskForce.
5 I. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, including RSA 188-F and RSA 674, the
6 authority may, in consultation with the community college system of New Hampshire and the
7 department of military affairs and veterans services, authorize, lease, or otherwise facilitate the
8 construction and operation of housing on the Great Bay community college property within the
9 Pease development area. Such housing may be designed to accommodate Great Bay community
10 collegestudents, faculty, and staff, and active or reserve military personnel assigned to duties in the
11 NewHampshirenationalguard.
12 II. Notwithstanding RSA 14:49, there is established a task force on housing at Great Bay
13 communitycollegetostudyandmakerecommendationsconcerningthefeasibilityofdevelopingsuch
14 housingfacilities. Thetaskforceshall:
15 (a) Consider community college system residence hall models, leased apartment models,
16 andmilitarydormitoryorhousingmodels.
17 (b) Review potential construction and operation funding sources, including subsidies,
18 grants,low-incomehousingprograms,orstateorfederalappropriations.
19 (c) Determine ownership and leasing models, construction financing mechanisms, and
20 revenuesourcessufficienttooperateandmaintainthefacility.
21 (d) Evaluatestaffingoptionsforresidentiallife,maintenance,andsecurity.
22 (e) Compile data on cost, occupancy, resident demographics, student outcomes,
23 affordability,andsustainability.
24 III. Thetaskforceshallincludethefollowingmembers:
25 (a) Onesenatorappointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
26 (b) Onerepresentativeappointedbythespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives.
27 (c) The commissioner of the department of military affairs and veterans services, or
28 designee.
29 (d) ThechancellorofthecommunitycollegesystemofNewHampshire,ordesignee.
SB490 -ASINTRODUCED
-Page2-
1 (e) TheexecutivedirectorofthePeasedevelopmentauthority,ordesignee.
2 (f) ThepresidentofGreatBaycommunitycollege,ordesignee.
3 IV. The task force shall submit a report with findings and any recommendations for
4 proposed legislation to the governor, the president of the senate, the speaker of the house of
5 representatives,andthecityofPortsmouthnolaterthanDecember30,2026.
6 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
