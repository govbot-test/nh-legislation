3 I. Themembersofthecommitteeshallbeasfollows:
4 (a) Onememberofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
5 (b) Three members of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the
6 houseofrepresentatives.

8 thedutiesofthecommittee.
9 3 Duties. Thecommitteeshallstudyschoolbullyingandmeanstopreventit.

11 among the members. The first meeting of the committee shall be called by the first-named senate

13 section. Threemembersofthecommitteeshallconstituteaquorum.

15 legislation to the president of the senate, the speaker of the house of representatives, the senate
16 clerk,thehouseclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
17 6 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
