SPONSORS: Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Birdsell, Dist 19; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Innis, Dist 7;
Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. McConkey, Dist 3; Sen. Lang,

Carson, Dist 14; Sen. Prentiss, Dist 5; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Perkins Kwoka,
Dist 21; Sen. Abbas, Dist 22; Rep. Moffett, Merr. 4; Rep. Wood, Merr. 13; Rep.
Aures,Merr.13
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill expands the list of eligible national guard service members who refer citizens for
enlistment in the national guard, increases the state cash incentive for those who re-enlist or
reclassify into a critical unfilled vacancy, and expands the incentive program to include additional
recipients for reimbursement for achieving or recertification of credentials and fees associated with
theirmilitaryjobspecialty.

BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Hampshire National Guard Enlistment and Reenlistment Incentive Program
2 Established. AmendRSA110-B:60toreadasfollows:
3 110-B:60 New Hampshire National Guard Enlistment and Reenlistment Incentive Program
4 Established. For the purpose of encouraging enlistment and reenlistment in the national guard
5 there is hereby established a New Hampshire national guard enlistment incentive program. This
6 program authorizes a cash incentive up to $1000 to any current or former members of the New
7 Hampshire national guard [in the pay grades of E-1 to O-3 or any former member of the New
8 Hampshire national guard] or any currently serving service member for each new or prior
9 service recruit that they bring into the New Hampshire national guard. The program also
10 authorizes a cash incentive up to [$6,000] $8,000 to current members of the New Hampshire
11 national guard who are not eligible for a federal reenlistment bonus and who reenlist for service in
12 the New Hampshire national guard, or who reclassify into a critical, unfilled vacancy. The
13 program authorizes reimbursement of expenses and fees up to $4,000 associated with
14 achieving or recertification of credentials applicable with their military job specialty for
15 members of the New Hampshire national guard who are not authorized federal
16 reimbursementforsuchexpenses.
17 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
