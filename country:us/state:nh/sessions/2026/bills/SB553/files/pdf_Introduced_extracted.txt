SB553-FN-ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION
26-2155
09/08
553-FN
ANACT relativetopenaltiesforattemptingtoeludepursuitbylawenforcement.
SPONSORS: Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Birdsell, Dist 19; Sen. Ricciardi, Dist 9; Sen. Carson,
Dist 14; Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Rep. Roy, Rock. 31; Rep. Gorski, Hills. 2; Rep. N.
Murphy,Hills.12;Rep.Crawford,Carr.3
COMMITTEE: Judiciary
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill increases the penalties for willfully attempting to elude pursuit by a law enforcement
officer by increasing speed, extinguishing headlamps while still in motion, or abandoning a vehicle
whilebeingpursued.

STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetopenaltiesforattemptingtoeludepursuitbylawenforcement.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 DisobeyinganOfficer;Penalty. AmendRSA265:4,IItoreadasfollows:
2 II. Any person who violates any provision of paragraph I of this section may have his or her
3 license or privilege to drive and any registrations issued in his or her name suspended. Any person
4 who violates the provision of subparagraph I(c) by willfully attempting to elude pursuit by
5 a law enforcement officer by increasing speed, extinguishing headlamps while still in
6 motion, or abandoning a vehicle while being pursued shall have such license or privilege
7 to drive and any registrations issued in his or her name suspended for a period of not less
8 than 6 months. In addition, any person who violates the provisions of subparagraphs I(a), (b), (d),
9 (e),or(f)ofthissectionshallbeguiltyofaclassAmisdemeanor.
10 2 New Paragraph; Disobeying an Officer; Penalty. Amend RSA 265:4 by inserting after
11 paragraphIIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
12 IV. In addition to the penalties listed in paragraphs II and III, any person who violates the
13 provision of subparagraph I(c) by willfully attempting to elude pursuit by a law enforcement officer
14 by increasing speed, extinguishing headlamps while still in motion, or abandoning a vehicle while
15 being pursued shall be sentenced to a term of at least 5 consecutive days in the house of corrections,
16 whichmaynotbesuspendedordeferred.
17 3 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
