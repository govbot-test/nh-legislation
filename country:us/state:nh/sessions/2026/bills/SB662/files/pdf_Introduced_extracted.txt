SB662-FN-A-ASINTRODUCED
26-3267
04/08
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT making an appropriation to the department of natural and cultural resources for
renovationstotheNorthwoodMeadowsLakeDam.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Appropriation; Department of Natural and Cultural Resources. There is hereby appropriated
2 to the department of natural and cultural resources the sum of $1,000,000 for the biennium ending
3 June 30, 2027, whichshall be nonlapsinguntil June 30, 2029, for thepurpose of making renovations
4 and repairs to the Northwood Meadows Lake Dam. The governor is authorized to draw a warrant
5 forsaidsumoutofanymoneyinthetreasurynototherwiseappropriated.
6 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
