IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT making an appropriation for each full-time student eligible for the in-state tuition
rateenrolledintheuniversitysystemofNewHampshire.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 NewSection;Education;StateCollegeandUniversitySystem;In-StateTuitionAppropriation.
2 AmendRSA187-Abyinsertingaftersection25-athefollowingnewsection:
3 187:25-b In-State Tuition; Appropriation. Beginning in fiscal year 2027 and for each fiscal year
4 thereafter, the sum of $300 for each full-time student in this state enrolled in the university system
5 of New Hampshire, who is also eligible for an in-state tuition rate is hereby appropriated to the
6 university system of New Hampshire for the purpose of reducing in-state tuition costs for qualifying
7 students. The number of full-time students enrolled in the university system of New Hampshire in
8 this state who are also eligible for an in-state tuition rate shall be calculated every October 1. The
9 total appropriation for each year of the biennium shall be limited to $5,400,000. These funds shall
10 benon-lapsing. The governor is authorized to draw a warrant for said sums out of any moneyin the
11 treasurynototherwiseappropriated.
12 2 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
