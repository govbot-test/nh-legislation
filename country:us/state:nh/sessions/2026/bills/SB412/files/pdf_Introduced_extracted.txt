ANACT relative to the conditional release of delinquent minors and children in need of

BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Delinquent Children; Definition of Conditional Release. Amend RSA 169-B:2, VIII(d) to read
2 asfollows:
3 (d) Returntothecourtfor violationofconditions ofthereleaseand changeof disposition
4 at any time during the term of conditional release. The court may retain jurisdiction to hear
5 violations of the conditions of release so long as the violation is filed within the period of
6 conditionalrelease.
7 2 New Paragraph; Delinquent Children; Jurisdiction. Amend RSA 169-B:4 by inserting after
8 paragraphVthefollowingnewparagraph:
9 V-a. The court may retain jurisdiction to hear violations of conditions of release pursuant to
10 RSA169-B:2,VIII(d).
11 3 ChildreninNeedof Services;DefinitionofConditionalRelease. AmendRSA169-D:2,III(d) to
12 readasfollows:
13 (d) Return to the court for violation of the conditions of the release and change of the
14 disposition at any time during the term of the conditional release. The court may retain
15 jurisdiction to hear violations of the conditions of release so long as the violation is filed
16 withintheperiodofconditionalrelease.
17 4 New Paragraph; Children in Need of Services; Jurisdiction. Amend RSA 169-D:3 by inserting
18 afterparagraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
19 II-a. The court may retain jurisdiction to hear violations of conditions of release pursuant to
20 RSA169-D:2,III(d).
21 5 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
