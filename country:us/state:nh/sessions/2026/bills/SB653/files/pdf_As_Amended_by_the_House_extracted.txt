AMENDEDANALYSIS
Thisbill:
I. Modifiestheprocedureforadopting,rescinding,andelectingamunicipalbudgetcommittee.
II. Modifies the time frame for the appointment of initial members of a cooperative school
districtbudgetcommittee.
III. Enablescountiestochangethedatesoftheirbiennialbudgets.

3 III. Voting shall be by official ballot unless the town has not adopted the official ballot, in

6 moderator for a period of not less than one hour following the completion of discussion on the

16 whoareelected,withtheinitialmembersappointedtoone-yeartermsbythemoderator?”
17 (c) Except for a town or district that uses an official ballot form of meeting under RSA
18 40:13 or under a charter adopted pursuant to RSA 49-D, if budget committee members-at-large are
19 to be elected with initial members-at-large elected to one-year terms pursuant to RSA 32:15, III, the
20 wording of the question shall be as follows: “To assist voters in the prudent appropriation of public
21 funds, shall we adopt the provisions of RSA 32:14 to establish a budget committee consisting of
22 (number) members-at-large who are elected, with the initial members elected to one-year terms by

25 under a charter adopted pursuant to RSA 49-D, if budget committee members-at-large are to be

27 pursuant to RSA 32:15, III, the wording of the question shall be as follows: “To assist voters in the
28 prudent appropriation of public funds, shall we adopt the provisions of RSA 32:14 to establish a
29 budget committee consisting of (number) members-at-large who are elected, with initial members
30 electedatthenextannualmeeting?”

8 follows:
9 III. If a town or district uses an official ballot form of meeting under RSA 40:13 or
10 under a charter adopted pursuant to RSA 49-D, and the meeting decides members-at-large
11 are to be elected, the meeting shall either elect the initial members-at-large for staggered
12 terms as described in paragraph II at the next annual meeting, or shall authorize the
13 moderator to appoint members-at-large to serve a one-year term until the next annual
14 meeting, as provided in RSA 669:17. Otherwise, if the meeting decides members-at-large are to

18 shall not begin until that next annual meeting, and shall be by official ballot if the municipality has
19 adopted the official ballot system, as set forth in RSA 669 or RSA 671. A budget committee shall
20 be deemed duly constituted and able to conduct business once a quorum of its elected or
21 appointedmembers-at-largehavebeenswornintooffice.

23 annual meeting to change the number or manner of selection of its members-at-large. No such
24 change shall take effect until the annual meeting following the meeting at which the change was
25 adopted. Currently elected or appointed members-at-large shall continue to serve until the
26 endoftheircurrentterm.
27 (a) To change the number of members-at-large, the wording of the question
28 shall be: “Shall we change the number of budget committee members-at-large from

36 may vote to establish a budget committee pursuant to RSA 32:14 and may rescind such action in a
37 like manner. The budget committee shall have the same number of members as the cooperative

1 district school board plus one additional member from the school board as provided in this
2 paragraph. The terms of office and manner of election of members shall be determined in the same
3 manner as for the cooperative school board. Whenever it is voted to establish a budget committee,
4 the moderator in the first instance shall appoint the members of the budget committee, except for
5 the additional member appointed from the school board, within [15] 30 days of the vote establishing
6 the committee. The members appointed by the moderator shall serve until the next annual meeting
7 when the meeting shall elect their successors. No member of the cooperative school board shall be

14 AmendRSA32byinsertingaftersection26thefollowingnewsection:
15 32:27 ProcedureforFiscalYearChange.
16 I. Upon an affirmative vote of a majority of county voters, a county may move the biennial
17 budget to the off year of the state election. Putting the question on the ballot shall require a vote in
18 the affirmative by a majority of the county commissioners. Said vote shall happen no later than the
19 September1beforethestateelections.
20 II. Notice of a vote of the county commissioners pursuant to paragraph I shall be given at
21 least 15 days, but not more than 60 days, prior, by a local newspaper of general circulation, public
22 notice on the county's main website or any social media accounts utilized by the county, and public
23 noticepostedinthe2placeswherethecountyregularlypostsnoticesofitsgoverningbodymeetings,
24 orbyanyothermeansdeemedappropriatebythecountycommissioners.
25 III. The question shall be put to the voters at the November state election and shall read as
26 follows:
27 "ShallNAMEOFCOUNTYmovetoabiennialbudgetcyclestartingintheyearYEARNUMBER?"
