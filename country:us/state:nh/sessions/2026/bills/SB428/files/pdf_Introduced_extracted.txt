SB428-ASINTRODUCED
26-2019
09/06
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to the maximum amount of terms an individual can serve on the
electricians'board.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Electricians;Board. AmendRSA319-C:4,IandI-atoreadasfollows:
2 I. There shall be an electricians' board consisting of 5 members, including 2 master
3 electricians, one journeyman electrician, and 2 public members, each to be appointed by the
4 governor, with the approval of the council, to a term of 5 years. No member of the board shall be
5 appointed to more than [2] 3 terms. The state fire marshal or his designee shall be an ex officio
6 memberoftheboard,butshallnotvoteinboarddeterminations.
7 I-a. No member of the board shall serve more than [12] 16 years, including the time of [2] 3
8 5-yearterms,beingheldoverafteratermexpires,andfulfillinganunexpiredterm. Members'terms
9 shallbestaggered and inno caseshall anymember's term coincidewithanother member's term. To
10 avoid coinciding terms, the next appointment for a member shall be for a term which complies with
11 thisparagraph.
12 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
