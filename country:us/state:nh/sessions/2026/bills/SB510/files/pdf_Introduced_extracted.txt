SB510 -ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION
26-2106
06/07
510
ANACT ensuring state-operated, publicly accessible spaces have designated smoking
areasundetectabletovisitors.
SPONSORS: Sen. Murphy, Dist 16; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Birdsell,
Dist19
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill requires that any designated smoking area on publicly-owned or publicly-operated
property accessible to the public is constructed or maintained so that cigarette smoke is not
detectable by sight or smell in any public or common area. It exempts properties or areas under
statecontrolthatarenotaccessibletothevisitingpublic.

ANACT ensuring state-operated, publicly accessible spaces have designated smoking

1 1 New Paragraph; Indoor Smoking Act; Smoking Prohibited. Amend RSA 155:66 by inserting
2 afterparagraphIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
3 II-a. Any publicly-owned or publicly-operated property accessible to the public shall provide,
4 if smoking is not entirely prohibited on the premises, a designated smoking area that is constructed
5 ormaintainedsothatsmokeisnotdetectableinanypublicorcommonarea. Thisprovisionshallnot
6 apply to publicly-owned property or areas under state control that are not accessible to the visiting
7 public.
8 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
