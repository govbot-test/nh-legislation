Sen.Rosenwald,Dist13;Rep.Potucek,Rock.13

ANALYSIS
This bill adds a definition of entity and explicitly includes entities in the prohibited conduct for
theoffenseofidentityfraud.

IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relativetotheoffenseofidentityfraud.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 IdentityFraud;Definitions. AmendRSA638:25toreadasfollows:
2 638:25 Definitions.
3 Inthissubdivision:
4 I. "Entity" means any corporation, limited liability company, trust, partnership,
5 incorporated or unincorporated association or organization, and any other legal entity or
6 combination of entities, including any state, county, or municipal government and any
7 subdivision,branch,department,oragencythereof.
8 II. "Personalidentifyinginformation"meansanyname,number,orinformationthatmaybe
9 used, alone or in conjunction with any other information, to assume the identity of an individual or
10 entity, including any name, address, telephone number, driver's license number, social security
11 number, employer or place of employment, employee identification number, mother's maiden name,
12 demand deposit account number, savings account number, credit card number, debit card number,
13 personal identification number, account number, [or] computer password identification, or logo or
14 othermarkusedtoidentifyanentity.
15 [II.] III. "Pose" means to falsely represent oneself, directly or indirectly, as another person
16 orpersons,orentityorentities.
17 [III.] IV. "Victim" means any person or entity whose personal identifying information has
18 been unlawfully obtained or recorded or any person or entity that provided money, credit, goods,
19 services, or anything of value and has suffered financial loss as a direct result of the commission or
20 attemptedcommissionofaviolationofthissubdivision.
21 2 IdentityFraud. AmendRSA638:26,Itoreadasfollows:
22 I. Apersonisguiltyofidentityfraudwhentheperson:
23 (a) Poses as another person or entity with the purpose to defraud in order to obtain
24 money,credit,goods,services,oranythingelseofvalue;
25 (b) Obtains, [or] records, or uses personal identifying information about another person
26 or entity withouttheexpressauthorizationof suchpersonor entity, withtheintent toposeas such
27 personorentity;
28 (c) Obtains, [or] records, or uses personal identifying information about a person or
29 entity without the express authorization of such person or entity in order to assist another to pose
30 assuchpersonorentity;or
SB622-FN-ASAMENDEDBYTHESENATE

1 (d) Poses as another person or entity, without the express authorization of such person
2 or entity, with the purpose of obtaining confidential information about such person or entity, or
3 anotherpersonorentity,thatisnotavailabletothegeneralpublic.
4 3 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJanuary1,2027.
