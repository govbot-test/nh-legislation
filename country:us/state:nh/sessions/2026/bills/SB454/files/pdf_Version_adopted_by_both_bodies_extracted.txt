STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to public health outreach by the department of health and human
servicesregardingawarenessofAlzheimer'sdiseaseandotherdementias.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Paragraph; Department of Health and Human Services; Public Health Outreach;
2 Awareness of Alzheimer's Disease and Other Dementias. Amend RSA 126-A:5 by inserting after
3 paragraphXXVIIthefollowingnewparagraph:
4 XXVII-a.(a) Subjecttoavailableresources, thecommissioner shalldirect thebureauof adult
5 and aging services to collaborate with the division of public health to update and incorporate into
6 existing relevant public health outreach programs, the following information electronically to aid
7 people's understanding and awareness of Alzheimer's disease and other dementias and to improve
8 publichealthregarding:
9 (1) Early signs of Alzheimer's disease and other dementias that should be discussed
10 withhealthcareprofessionals.
11 (2) Thevalueofearlydetectionanddiagnosis.
12 (3) The relationship between Alzheimer’s disease, dementia, and other chronic
13 healthconditions.
14 (4) Lifestyleinterventionsthat promotebrainhealthand reducetheriskof cognitive
15 decline.
16 (b) The bureau of adult and aging services shall collaborate with the division of public
17 health to incorporate in existing relevant public health outreach programs information to educate
18 healthcareproviderson:
19 (1) The importance of risk reduction, early detection, and timely diagnosis of
20 cognitiveimpairment.
21 (2) Validated cognitive assessment tools and biomarker tests for the detection and
22 diagnosisofcognitiveimpairment.
23 (3) The value of a Medicare annual wellness visit for cognitive health and the
24 Medicarecareplanningbillingcodesforindividualslivingwithcognitiveimpairment.
25 (4) InformationregardingFDA-approvedtreatments.
26 (5) Resources available to individuals living with dementia and their families and
27 caregivers.
28 (c) Thebureauofadultandagingservicesshallcompileareportevery2yearsregarding
29 the incorporation of Alzheimer’s and dementia into the state public outreach programs that shall be
SB454-FN-VERSIONADOPTEDBYBOTHBODIES
-Page2-
1 submitted to the subcommittee on Alzheimer’s disease and other related dementias, established in
2 RSA126-A:15-a,bySeptember1,2027andbi-annuallythereafter.
3 (d) It is the intent of the legislature that the department apply for available federal or
4 private funds when such funds are appropriate and relevant to supporting the state’s efforts to
5 implement this paragraph or the Healthy Brain Initiative Road Map, as described by the National
6 Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, as available resources and eligibility permit. Any such
7 federal funds obtained shall be utilized to support the state’s efforts to fully integrate brain health
8 into public-health practice and reduce the risk and impact of Alzheimer’s disease and other
9 dementias.
10 2 EffectiveDate.Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
