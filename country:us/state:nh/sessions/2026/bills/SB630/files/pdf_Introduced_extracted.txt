COMMITTEE: Transportation
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill lowers the minimum speed limit that local authorities can declare as a reasonable and
safespeedlimitincertainareasfrom25milesperhourto20milesperhour.
Thisbillisarequestofthedepartmentoftransportation.
