STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishingaNewHampshire-Greecetradecouncil.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Section; New Hampshire-Greece Trade Council. Amend RSA 12-O by inserting after
2 section23-athefollowingnewsection:
3 12-O:23-b NewHampshire-GreeceTradeCouncil.
4 I. Thegeneralcourtfindsasfollows:
5 (a) Greek-AmericanswereanimportantpartofNewHampshire'searlyhistory.
6 (b) Greece, officially known as the Hellenic Republic, is one of New Hampshire’s largest
7 tradepartners.
8 (c) New Hampshire should build upon its historic, cultural, and economic ties with the
9 HellenicRepublic.
10 (d) New Hampshire has the highest per capita concentration of Greek Americans in the
11 country.
12 II. The commissioner of the department of business and economic affairs and the secretary
13 of state shall have the authority to establish a New Hampshire-Greece trade council to meet with
14 one or more trade councils established in the Hellenic Republic. The purpose of the council shall be
15 to advance bilateral trade and investment between New Hampshire and Greece, initiate joint action
16 on policy issues of mutual interest to New Hampshire and Greece, and promote business and
17 academicexchangesbetweenNewHampshireandGreece.
18 III. Themembersofthecouncilshallinclude:
19 (a) Twosenators,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
20 (b) Twohousemembers,appointedbythespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives.
21 (c) The commissioner of the department of business and economic development, or their
22 designee.
23 (d) Thesecretaryofstate,ortheirdesignee.
24 (e) A representative from the business and industry association, appointed by that
25 organization.
26 (f) A representative of the division of economic development, appointed by the
27 commissionerofthedepartmentofbusinessandeconomicaffairs.
28 (g) A representative of the university system of New Hampshire, appointed by the
29 chancellor.
30 (h) Twopublicmembers,appointedbythegovernor.
31 IV. Thecouncilshallelectachairperson,whosedutiesshallincludecallingmeetings.
SB526-VERSIONADOPTEDBYBOTHBODIES
-Page2-
1 V. The New Hampshire-Greece trade council shall be authorized to raise and accept funds,
2 through direct solicitation or other fundraising events, alone, or with other groups, and accept gifts,
3 grants, and bequests from individuals, corporations, foundations, government agencies, and public
4 andprivateorganizationsandinstitutions.
5 VI. There is hereby established in the state treasury the New Hampshire-Greece trade
6 council fund. The funds shall be comprised of monies obtained according to paragraph V, and shall
7 be used to defray the council’s administrative costs and to carry out its purposes. The fund shall be
8 non-lapsingandshallbecontinuallyappropriatedtothecouncil.
9 2 New Subparagraph; Application of Receipts; New Hampshire-Greece trade council fund.
10 AmendRSA6:12,I(b)byinsertingaftersubparagraph(410)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
11 (411) Moneys deposited in the New Hampshire-Greece trade council fund as
12 establishedinRSA12-O:23-b.
13 3 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
