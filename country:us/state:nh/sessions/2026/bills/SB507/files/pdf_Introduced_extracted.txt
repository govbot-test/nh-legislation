ANACT relativetothefinancialobligationofaschooldistrictconcerningstudentsexpelled
forcommittingassaultagainstaschoolstaffmember.
SPONSORS: Sen.Murphy,Dist16; Sen.Sullivan,Dist 18;Sen.Avard,Dist 12;Sen.Innis, Dist
7;Sen.Pearl,Dist17
COMMITTEE: EducationFinance
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill limits a school district's financial obligations to the continuing education of an expelled
pupil totheaveragecost-per-pupil where thepupil is expelled as theresult of committingan assault
againstaschoolstaffmember.
