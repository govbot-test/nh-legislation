SB581-FN-ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION
26-2018
12/09
581-FN
ANACT modifying education freedom account (EFA) eligibility and removing priority
guidelinesandtheenrollmentcaprelativetoEFAeligibility.
SPONSORS: Sen.Sullivan,Dist18;Rep.Noble,Hills.2
COMMITTEE: EducationFinance
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill repeals the priority guidelines and the enrollment cap for education freedom accounts
(EFAs). The bill also removes references to both priority guidelines and the enrollment cap relative
toapplicationsforEFAs.

SB581-FN-ASINTRODUCED
26-2018
12/09
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT modifying education freedom account (EFA) eligibility and removing priority
guidelinesandtheenrollmentcaprelativetoEFAeligibility.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Inclusionof PriorityGuideline Students inEnrollment Cap. Amend RSA194-F:3, Ito read as
2 follows:
3 I. A parent may apply to the scholarship organization to establish an EFA for an eligible
4 student. The scholarship organization shall accept and approve applications for enrollment on a
5 rolling basis each year [and shall establish procedures for approving applications for enrollment in
6 an expeditious manner. Priority guideline students shall not be subject to the enrollment cap and
7 shall be enrolled on a rolling basis]. The scholarship organization shall establish procedures
8 forapprovingapplicationsforenrollmentinanexpeditiousmanner.
9 2 RemovalofIncreaseinEnrollmentCap. AmendRSA194-F:3,I-atoreadasfollows:
10 I-a. For the 2025-2026 school year, the enrollment cap shall be 10,000. [For each
11 subsequent year, if the total enrollment of the prior year is greater than 90 percent of the prior
12 year's enrollment cap, then the enrollment cap shall be increased once by 25 percent when
13 enrollment begins for the year. The department of education shall publish on its website
14 informationidentifyingtheenrollmentcapwhenitisincreasedpursuanttothisparagraph.]
15 3 Repeal. Thefollowingarerepealed:
16 I. RSA194-F:1,XIII,relativetothedefinitionofpriorityguidelines.
17 II. RSA194-F:1,XIV, relativetothedefinitionofenrollmentcap.
18 III. RSA194-F:1-b,relativetoenrollmentprioritization.
19 IV. 2025, 75:6 and 7, relative to the contingent repeal of the enrollment cap and priority
20 guidelines based on certification by the department of education that student applications for the
21 EFAprogramhavenotexceededtheenrollmentcapfor2consecutiveschoolyears.
22 4 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
