SB496 -ASINTRODUCED
2026SESSION

07/09
496
ANACT relative to supervision and registration requirements for associates of broker-
dealers.
SPONSORS: Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Watters,
Dist4;Sen.Gannon,Dist23;Sen.Innis,Dist7;Rep.Miles,Hills.12
COMMITTEE: Commerce
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill exempts certain private residences designated by broker-dealers as a supervisory
locationfrombeingclassifiedasabranchoffice.

SB496-ASINTRODUCED
26-2061
07/09
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to supervision and registration requirements for associates of broker-
dealers.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Securities; Uniform Securities Act; Definitions. Amend RSA 421-B:1-102, 5(B) to read as
2 follows:
3 (B) Notwithstandingtheexclusions providedinsubsection(5)(A)(ii),anylocation
4 that is responsible for supervising the activities of agents of the broker dealer at one or more non-
5 branch locations of the broker-dealer shall be a branch office. Notwithstanding the foregoing, a
6 private residence designated by a broker-dealer as a residential supervisory location in
7 compliance with FINRA Rule 3110.19, or successor rule, shall not be deemed a branch
8 officeforpurposesofthischapter.
9 2 NewSubparagraph; Securities;UniformSecuritiesAct; Definitions. AmendRSA421-B:1-102,
10 5byinsertingaftersubparagraph(C)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
11 (D) “Residential supervisory location” means a private residence from which an
12 associated person engages in supervisory functions that a broker-dealer has designated as a
13 residentialsupervisorylocationincompliancewithFINRARule3110.19,orsuccessorrule.
14 3 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
