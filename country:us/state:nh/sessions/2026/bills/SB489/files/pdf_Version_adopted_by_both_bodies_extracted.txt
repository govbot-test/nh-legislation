STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT alteringtheappointmentprocessfortheboardoftaxandlandappeals.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Board of Tax and Land Appeals; Appointment; Term; Chairman. Amend RSA 71-B:2 to read
2 asfollows:
3 71-B:2 Appointment; Term; Chairman. The members of the board shall be appointed by and
4 serveat thepleasureof the governor and council, rather thanfixed terms. The governor and council
5 shall appoint one member as chairman to serve in that capacity for the duration of his or her term.
6 The executive council shall hold a hearing prior to confirmation according to the procedures under
7 RSA4:44.
8 2 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
