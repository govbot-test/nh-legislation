SB604-FN-A-ASINTRODUCED
26-2258
07/08
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT makinganappropriationtotheuniversitysystemofNewHampshire.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Appropriation; University System of New Hampshire. For the fiscal year ending June 30,
2 2027, the sum of $14,000,000 is appropriated to the university system of New Hampshire for the
3 purpose of restoring funding to university system that was reduced pursuant to 2025, 140 (HB 1).
4 The governor is authorized to draw a warrant for said sum out of any money in the treasury not
5 otherwiseappropriated.
6 2 Appropriation; Community College System of New Hampshire. For the fiscal year ending
7 June 30, 2027, the sum of $3,500,000 is appropriated to the community college system of New
8 Hampshirefor thepurpose of lowering credit-hour tuition rates to continue the previous freeze. The
9 governor is authorized to draw a warrant for said sum out of any money in the treasury not
10 otherwiseappropriated.
11 3 EffectiveDate. ThisactshalltakeeffectJuly1,2026.
