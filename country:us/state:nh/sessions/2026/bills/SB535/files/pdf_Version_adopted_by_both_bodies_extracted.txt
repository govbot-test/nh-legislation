ANACT defining residential breeder and imported animal for the purposes of animal
transfersandremovingreferencestocommercialkennels.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Paragraphs; Definitions. Amend RSA 437:1 by inserting after paragraph V the following
2 newparagraphs:
3 VI. “Residential breeder” means an individual who owns and breeds dogs or cats within
4 their primary residence and not in a separate breeding facility. A residential breeder’s operations
5 shall be integrated into their home environment and shall transfer no more than 50 dogs or cats
6 with or without a fee or donation, between January 1 and December 31 of any calendar year. A
7 residential breeder shall raise the litters in a household setting by maintaining the animals as part
8 of the family environment. A residential breeder shall not transfer imported animals within New
9 Hampshirewithor without a feeor donation. Residential breeders shall be exempt from compliance
10 with rules adopted by the department relative to premises requirements to house, harbor, or display
11 animalsinNewHampshire.
12 VII. “Imported animal" means any dog, cat, ferret, or other live animal or bird customarily
13 used as household pets that is brought into this state, as well as the offspring of any animal
14 imported into the state while pregnant. Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, any
15 animal imported prior to birth shall be considered to have met the import requirements of this
16 chapterifthepregnantfemalehasalsometthoserequirements.
17 2 GroupLicenses. AmendRSA466:6,Itoreadasfollows:
18 I. The owner or keeperof 5or moredogsshall annuallybyApril 30paytherequired feeand
19 obtainalicenseauthorizingtheownerorkeepertokeepthedogsuponthepremisesdescribedinthe
20 license,oroffthepremiseswhileunder suchowner'sorkeeper's control. Suchownerorkeeper shall
21 not be required to obtain a ["commercial kennel"] "pet vendor" license under RSA 466:4, III unless
22 such person [has a commercial kennel] is a pet vendor as defined under [RSA 466:4, III] RSA
23 437:1,IV.
24 3 Fees;PetVendors. AmendRSA466:4,IIItoreadasfollows:
25 III. Fees for dogs licensed [in a commercial kennel] as a pet vendor shall be based on the
26 numbers of dogs licensed, as in RSA 466:6 for group licenses. For purposes of this paragraph,
27 ["commercial kennel"] "pet vendor" shall have the same meaning as RSA 437:1, IV [means the
28 establishment or domicile of any personwho sells dogs at wholesale or retail; and, if retail, who sells
29 or transfers 10 or more litters per year, or sells or transfers 50 or more puppies per year; or who

1 derives 40 percent or more of gross annual income from the sale or transfer of dogs]. The owner or
2 keeper of any dog licensed under this paragraph shall not be assessed a companion animal
3 populationcontrolfee.
4 4 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
