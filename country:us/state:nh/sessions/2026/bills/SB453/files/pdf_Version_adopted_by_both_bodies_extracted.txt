453
ANACT authorizing advanced practice registered nurses and physician associates to make
certaincertifications.
SPONSORS: Sen.Prentiss,Dist5;Sen.Rosenwald,Dist13;Sen.Watters,Dist4;Sen.Birdsell,
Dist19;Sen.Avard,Dist12;Sen.Perkins Kwoka,Dist21;Sen.Lang,Dist2; Sen.
Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Ward, Dist 8; Sen. Sullivan, Dist 18;
Sen. McGough, Dist 11; Sen. Murphy, Dist 16; Sen. Innis, Dist 7; Sen. Rochefort,
Dist1;Sen.Fenton,Dist10
COMMITTEE: HealthandHumanServices
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill adds advanced practice registered nurses and physician associates as medical professionals
whocan:
I. Document a lasting medical necessity, which allows for a vehicle equipment waiver for
personswithdisabilitiestobegrantedfor4yearsinsteadof2years;and
II. Exempt a child from certain required immunizations if they find that the immunization may
bedetrimentaltothechild'shealth.

7 2 Communicable Disease; Immunization Exemption. Amend RSA 141-C:20-c, I to read as
8 follows:
9 I. AphysicianlicensedunderRSA329,oraphysicianexemptedunderRSA329:21,III,an
10 advancedpracticeregisterednurselicensedunderRSA326-B,oraphysicianassociate
11 licensedunderRSA328-Dcertifiesthatimmunizationagainstaparticulardiseasemaybe
12 detrimental to the child's health. The exemption shall exist only for the length of time, in the
13 opinion of the physician, advanced practice registered nurse, or physician associate, such
14 immunization would be detrimental to the child. An exemption from immunization for one disease
15 shallnotaffectotherrequiredimmunizations.
16 3 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffect60daysafteritspassage.
