SB491-FN-FISCALNOTE
ASAMENDEDBYTHESENATE(AMENDMENT#2026-0379s)
ANACT enabling students to utilize education freedom account funds to pay for certain
careerandtechnicaleducationfunding.
FISCALIMPACT:
This bill requires the Department of Education to establish formulas and separate funds for
career and technical education (CTE) tuition and transportation costs, and to determine those
costs annually. This would be accomplished presumably through administrative rulemaking.
This bill also requires that Education Freedom Account (EFA) students in grades 9 through 12
beprovidedinformationaboutenrollinginCTEprograms.
Thisbillisnotexpectedtohaveafiscalimpact.
