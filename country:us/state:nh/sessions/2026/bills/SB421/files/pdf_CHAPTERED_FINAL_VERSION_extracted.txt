ANACT relative to the membership and duties of the trauma medical review committee and
establishing a study committee to review the membership and duties of other boards
andanappointmentrelatedtoemergencymedicalservices.
SPONSORS: Sen. Prentiss, Dist 5; Sen. Lang, Dist 2; Sen. Watters, Dist 4; Sen. Carson, Dist 14;
Sen. Fenton, Dist 10; Sen. Avard, Dist 12; Sen. Rosenwald, Dist 13; Sen. Gannon,
Dist 23; Sen. Pearl, Dist 17; Sen. Perkins Kwoka, Dist 21; Sen. McGough, Dist 11;
Sen.Birdsell,Dist19
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill amends the membership of and adds an additional duty to the trauma medical review
committeeandestablishesastudycommitteetoreviewthemembership,duties,andproceduresforother
boardsandanappointmentrelatedtoemergencymedicalservices.

1 124:1 TraumaMedicalReviewCommittee;Membership. AmendRSA153-A:8,Itoreadasfollows:
2 I. Thereisestablishedatraumamedicalreviewcommitteewhichshallconsistof:
3 (a) Onerepresentativefromeachtraumahospitallevelofcare,levelsI,II,III,andIV.These
4 representativesmayconsistoftraumasurgeons,emergency[Aminimumof5physiciansrepresentingthe
5 surgical disciplines of neurosurgery, general surgery, pediatric surgery, orthopedic surgery, and other]
6 physicians experienced in the treatment of adult and pediatric trauma patients, trauma program
7 managers,ortraumaregistrars.
8 (b) One member from the New Hampshire chapter of the American College of Emergency
9 Physicians.
10 (c) OnememberfromtheNewHampshireParamedicAssociation.
11 (d) OnememberfromtheNewHampshireEmergencyNursesAssociation.
12 (e) Onetraumanursecoordinatorwithabackgroundintraumaperformanceimprovement.
13 (f) OnememberfromtheNewHampshireAssociationofEmergencyMedicalTechnicians.
14 (g) Thestatemedicalexaminerordesignee.
15 (h) Onememberoftheemergencymedicalservicesmedicalcontrolboard.
16 (i) One representative from a New Hampshire acute care hospital nominated by the New
17 HampshireHospitalAssociation.
18 (j) OnerepresentativeoftheProfessionalFirefightersofNewHampshire.
19 (k) OnerepresentativeoftheNewHampshireAssociationofFireChiefs.
20 (l)Oneinjurypreventioncoordinator.
21 (m)Onetraumasurvivor.
22 (n)AphysicianorphysiciandesigneefromarehabilitationcenterinNewHampshirespecific
23 tothetreatmentofphysicalinjuriesfromtrauma.
24 (o)ArepresentativefromtheNewHampshireofficeofhighwaysafety.
25 124:2 New Subparagraph; Trauma Medical Review Committee; Duty Added. Amend RSA 153-A:8,
26 Vbyinsertingaftersubparagraph(h)thefollowingnewsubparagraph:
27 (i) Annually provide a report to the governor on the status of New Hampshire’s trauma
28 system including the names, levels, and locations of all trauma hospitals, the activities and
29 recommendations of the trauma medical review committee for the continuous improvement of New
30 Hampshire’s trauma system, and data that summarizes the leading causes of injury and death in New
31 Hampshirefrombothintentionalandunintentionalinjuries.

1 124:3 Committee Established. There is established a committee to study the composition, duties,
2 andresponsibilitiesofcertainboards.
3 124:4 MembershipandCompensation.
4 I. Themembersofthecommitteeshallbeasfollows:
5 (a) Twomembersofthehouseofrepresentatives.
6 (b) Onememberofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
7 II. Members of the committee shall receive mileage at the legislative rate when attending to the
8 dutiesofthecommittee.
9 124:5 Duties. Thecommittee'sstudyshallinclude,butnotbelimitedto:
10 I. Thecompositionanddutiesofthe:
11 (a) Emergencymedicalandtraumaservicescoordinatingboard,asestablishedbyRSA153-
12 A:3.
13 (b) Emergencymedicalservicesmedicalcontrolboard,asestablishedbyRSA153-A:5.
14 II. Theprocessfortheappointmentofthestatemedicaldirectorforemergencymedicalservices,
15 asestablishedinRSA153-A:7,II(c).
16 124:6 Chairperson; Quorum. The members of the study committee shall elect a chairperson from
17 among the members. The first meeting of the committee shall be called by the first-named house
18 member. The first meeting of the committee shall be held within 45 days of the effective date of this
19 section. Twomembersofthecommitteeshallconstituteaquorum.
20 124:7 Report. The committee shall report its findings and any recommendations for proposed
21 legislationtothespeakerofthehouseofrepresentatives,thepresidentofthesenate,thehouseclerk,the
22 senateclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
124:8 EffectiveDate. Thisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
Approved:June05,2026
EffectiveDate:June05,2026
