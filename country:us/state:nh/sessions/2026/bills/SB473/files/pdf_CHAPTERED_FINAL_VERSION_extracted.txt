ANACT definingtheterm"fieldofficers"withinthenationalguard.
SPONSORS: Sen.McGough,Dist11;Sen.Watters,Dist4;Sen.McConkey,Dist3;Sen.Pearl,Dist
17;Rep.Giasson,Hills.29;Rep.Miles,Hills.12;Rep.Moffett,Merr.4
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
This bill defines the term "field grade officers" or "field officers" for purposes of promotion and
appointment within the army national guard to mean officers holding the rank of lieutenant colonel or
colonel.
