ANACT establishing a commission to study the efficiency and structure of school
administrativeunits.
SPONSORS: Sen.Innis,Dist7;Sen.Watters,Dist4;Sen.McGough,Dist11;Sen.Lang,Dist2;
Sen.Avard,Dist12;Sen.Birdsell,Dist19;Sen.Ward,Dist8;Sen.Rochefort,Dist

Prentiss,Dist5;Sen.Murphy,Dist16; Sen.Carson,Dist14;Sen.McConkey, Dist
3;Sen.Pearl,Dist17;Rep.Thibault,Merr.25
COMMITTEE: Education
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AMENDEDANALYSIS
Thisbillestablishesacommissionwithinthedepartmentofeducationtostudytheefficiencyand
structureofschool administrativeunits (SAUs)for thepurposes ofidentifyingopportunitiesfor SAU
consolidationandrestructuringaswellaspropertytaxrelief.

SB574-ASAMENDEDBYTHESENATE
03/12/2026 1011s 26-2004
12/09
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT establishing a commission to study the efficiency and structure of school
administrativeunits.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 New Section; School Administrative Unit Study Commission. Amend RSA 194-C by inserting
2 aftersection12thefollowingnewsection:
3 194-C:13 SchoolAdministrativeUnitStudyCommission.
4 I. There is established a commission to study the efficiency of existing school system
5 structures. Thecommissionshallbeadministrativelyattachedtothedepartmentofeducation.
6 II. NotwithstandingRSA14:49,thecommissionshallconsistofthefollowingmembers:
7 (a) Twomembersofthesenate,appointedbythepresidentofthesenate.
8 (b) Three members of the house of representatives, appointed by the speaker of the
9 houseofrepresentatives.
10 (c) Thecommissionerofthedepartmentofeducation,ordesignee.
11 (d) A superintendent, appointed by the New Hampshire School Administrators
12 Association.
13 (e) A member of a local school board, appointed by the New Hampshire School Boards
14 Association.
15 (f) A business office administrator, appointed by the New Hampshire Association of
16 SchoolBusinessOfficials.
17 III. Legislativemembersofthecommissionshallreceivemileageatthelegislativeratewhen
18 attendingtothedutiesofthecommission.
19 IV. Thecommissionshall:
20 (a) Reviewexistingdemographicandenrollmentshiftsstatewide;
21 (b) Identify the number of school administrative units (SAUs) statewide, the cost per
22 pupil in each SAU, and how SAUs can adjust their resource allocations to help reduce property
23 taxes;
24 (c) Identifystatesthathaveconsolidatedorrestructuredtheirschoolsystems;
25 (d) Examineimpedimentstoconsolidationsandpotentialschoolclosures;and
26 (e) Solicit testimony from any person or organization with relevant information or
27 expertise.
28 V. The members of the study commission shall elect a chairperson from among the
29 members. The first meeting of the commission shall be called by the first-named senate member.
SB574-ASAMENDEDBYTHESENATE
-Page2-
1 The first meeting of the commission shall be held within 30 days of the effective date of this section.
2 Fivemembersofthecommissionshallconstituteaquorum.
3 VI. The commission shall report its findings and any recommendations for proposed
4 legislation to the speaker of the house of representatives, the president of the senate, the house
5 clerk,thesenateclerk,thegovernor,andthestatelibraryonorbeforeNovember1,2026.
6 2 Prospective Repeal. RSA 194-C:13, relative to the school administrative unit study
7 commission,isrepealed.
8 3 EffectiveDate.
9 I. Section2ofthisactshalltakeeffectonNovember1,2026.
10 II. Theremainderofthisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
