610-FN
ANACT allowing the insurance commissioner to approve innovative short or long-term care
policies.
SPONSORS: Sen. Ricciardi, Dist 9; Sen. Lang, Dist 2; Sen. Gannon, Dist 23; Sen. Watters, Dist 4;
Rep.Post,Hills.42;Rep.Spier,Hills.6;Rep.Burroughs,Carr.2
COMMITTEE: HealthandHumanServices
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AMENDEDANALYSIS
This bill allows the insurance commissioner to approve innovative short or long-term care policies or
certificatesandratesthatthecommissionerfindsarereasonableandinthepublicinterest.

6 (b) Thebenefitsarereasonableinrelationtothepremiumcharged.
