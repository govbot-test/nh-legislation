ANACT relative to the composition of the board of dental examiners and relative to
temporarylicensesforout-of-stateprofessionals.

17
COMMITTEE: ExecutiveDepartmentsandAdministration
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYSIS
Thisbill:
I. Mandatesthatonememberoftheboardofdentalexaminersbeageneraldentist.
II. Extends therulemakingauthorityof theofficeof professionallicensure and certificationas it
relates to the issuance of temporary licenses for out-of-state professionals from health care
professionalstoanyprofessionregulatedbyaboardortheoffice.

26-2172
09/06
STATEOFNEWHAMPSHIRE
IntheYearofOurLordTwoThousandTwenty-Six
ANACT relative to the composition of the board of dental examiners and relative to
temporarylicensesforout-of-stateprofessionals.
BeitEnactedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentativesinGeneralCourtconvened:
1 1 Dentists and Dentistry; Examining Board; Membership. Amend RSA 317-A:2, I to read as
2 follows:
3 I. There shall be a board of dental examiners consisting of 9 members,[;] including 6
4 dentists, 2 dental hygienists, and one public member, each to be appointed by the governor, with the
5 approval of the council, to a term of 5 years. One dentist member shall have a current permit to
6 administer general anesthesia and deep sedation, and one dentist member shall be a general
7 dentist. No member of the board shall be appointed to more than 2 consecutive terms. Only board
8 membersprovidedforinthisparagraphshallhavetheauthoritytovoteinboarddeterminations.
9 2 Office of Professional Licensure and Certification; Rulemaking Authority. Amend RSA 310:6,
10 Vtoreadasfollows:
11 V. Temporary licenses to out-of-state [health care] professionals who present evidence of an
12 active license in good standing from another jurisdiction in a profession regulated by a board or
13 the office. The temporary license shall be valid for 120 days, or until the office acts on an
14 application for full licensure, whichever happens first, and shall not be renewed, except that a
15 complete application for full licensure before the expiration of the temporary license shall continue
16 the validity of the temporary license until the office has acted on the application. All individuals
17 licensedunderrulesadoptedpursuanttothissubparagraphshallbesubjecttothejurisdictionofthe
18 statelicensingbodyforthatprofession.
19 3 EffectiveDate.
20 I. Section1ofthisactshalltakeeffectSeptember1,2026.
21 II. Theremainderofthisactshalltakeeffectuponitspassage.
